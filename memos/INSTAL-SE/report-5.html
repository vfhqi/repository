1. COMPANY DESCRIPTION & CONTEXT

Business Overview

Instalco AB is a Nordic technical installation and service provider, headquartered in Sweden. Core services include:
- Heating and plumbing
- Electrical installations
- Ventilation, cooling, and air systems
- Industrial solutions

Geographic footprint: Primary presence in Sweden (largest revenue contributor); material operations in Norway and Finland. The company operates a decentralised, acquisition-led growth model—typical of Nordic installation services roll-up players (peer set: Bravida, Caverion, Assemblin).

Business Model

Instalco generates revenue via two channels:
1. Project installations (new construction, renovations)
2. Service and maintenance (recurring, higher-margin revenue; 38% of net sales in Q4 2025)

Margin profile typical for installation services: low-double-digit EBIT margins; heavy labour content; tight working capital (invoice-to-cash cycles favourable).

Market Positioning

Estimated Nordic market (Sweden, Norway, Finland combined): SEK 214 billion. Instalco's implied share: approximately 6.4% based on SEK 13.6bn 2025 revenues. Market is highly fragmented; consolidation ongoing (Assemblin-Caverion merger 2024; Instalco's Fabri entry into Germany 2025).

Key competitors:
- Bravida (SEK ~17-18bn revenue; #1-2 Nordic by scale)
- Assemblin-Caverion (SEK ~43bn combined; #1 post-merger; ~21k employees)
- Multiple smaller, regional players

Management has implemented an operational excellence program (Instalco 2.0) focused on margin expansion over volume, targeting 8% EBITA vs. current 5.9%. Recovery in Nordic construction cycle is anticipated for 2026; order backlog is robust (SEK 9B+, +5.8% YoY); cash generation is strong (OCF SEK 1,010M, 126% conversion).
