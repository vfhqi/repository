Section 3 – Industry Structure + Concentration + Value Chain Economics 42 findings

Section summary
  1. highly fragmented European scrap market is driven by decarbonisation mandates, with regulatory retention keeping local scrap within Continental borders
  2. volatile Turkish import scrap indices dictate domestic pricing, while processing complexity determines the margin split between ferrous and non- ferrous
  3. low consolidation at the collection level leaves the top players exposed to aggressive volume-over-ROCE competition from local operators
  4. elevated Chinese steel exports and volatile European energy costs squeeze electric arc furnace margins, dampening immediate scrap demand
