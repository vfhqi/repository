  • Target state: ~63-65% gross margin by FY2026E; implies COGS per unit declining at approximately 4-6% per annum as volumes compound [#A]

Bull — historical:
- Bull — historical: ALK-abello achieved gross margins above 60% in 2017-2018 before the SLIT investment cycle diluted margins; the 63%+ target is a recovery, not an unprecedented level [#A]

  • Bull — initiatives: Hørsholm manufacturing modernisation programme (visible in capex disclosures) is designed to reduce batch costs; management guided for unit cost deflation in 2024 CMD materials [#A]

  • Bull — competitive: Biological allergen extract manufacturing has meaningful barriers; once at scale, COGS per unit is relatively stable (raw material is allergen extract — agricultural commodity but stable in cost) [#A]

Bear — historical drag:
- Bear — historical drag: ALK-abello's gross margin recovery has been slower than initially guided; 2022 guidance implied 60%+ gross margin by 2024E but realised FY2024 margin is likely still sub-60% based on trend [#A]

  • Sub: ❌ Three-year track record of gross margin undershooting guidance is a material caution flag against embedding full recovery in the base case

  • Bear — execution: SLIT tablet manufacturing involves biological extract standardisation; batch variability can impair yield, adding cost volatility not captured in unit cost projections [#A]

  • Bear — structural: The Hørsholm facility footprint is sized for a significantly higher volume than current; fixed cost absorption requires volume doubling — a long-dated event [#A]

  • Bear — SS skeptic: Berenberg and Nordea (per publicly available notes) have flagged that EBIT margin projections embed optimistic gross margin assumptions; the most bearish EBIT scenario (approximately 5-7% EBIT margin by 2026E) is driven by gross margin staying flat [#A]

  • Acceleration needed: Low acceleration required versus recent trend IF SLIT volumes sustain; but Driver 3 (gross margin) is conditionally dependent on Driver 1 (volume) — correlation is high [#J]

  • Key dependency: SLIT volume CAGR ≥10% sustained for 24M; no manufacturing disruption at Hørsholm; allergen extract raw material costs stable [#A]

  • Downside scenario: Gross margin flat at 57% for 2 years — EBIT margin stays approximately 5-7%; EPS 40-50% below bull case; IRR case breaks or requires multiple expansion to compensate [#J]
