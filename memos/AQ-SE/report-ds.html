1.

2.

3.
Q1 revenue miss - currency headwind: First-quarter 2026 net sales came in at SEK 2,358 million, undershooting DNB Carnegie's forecast of SEK 2,408 million by 2% due to a negative currency translation effect of -4.8%
4.
Q1 EBIT delivery - marginal miss: Adjusted EBIT for Q1 2026 was SEK 225 million, missing the sell-side consensus of SEK 227 million by 1%, though operating margin expanded to 9.6% due to lower raw material and personnel costs

Deliveries and execution - speed metrics: On-time delivery performance reached 96% in December 2025, validating management's emphasis on operational efficiency amidst scaling volumes
9.
Marine segment slowdown - market shift: Lower sales for marine transformers compared to the all-time high levels seen in 2024 and 2025 represented a drag on structural growth
