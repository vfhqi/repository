Key Takeaways

Fully Integrated Value Chain Model: Lerøy operates a complete vertical integration from roe to retail, processing 350,000-400,000 tonnes annually across three segments—Farming, Wild Catch, and VAPS&D—providing crucial diversification against cyclical biological risks that plague pure-play salmon farmers.
