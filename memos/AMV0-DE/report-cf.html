Aumovio SE represents a newly independent automotive supplier executing an aggressive restructuring program while navigating severe cyclical headwinds and the industry's disruptive transition to software-defined vehicles. The company emerged from Continental AG through a September 2025 spin-off carrying substantial operational bloat, including 84 manufacturing locations and a legacy cost structure that historically generated negative free cash flow throughout the 2020-2024 period under parent ownership. Management has since reduced the global headcount by more than 20,000 employees between 2023 and 2025 , targeting a net R&D-to-sales ratio below 10% by 2027 through multiple workforce reduction waves. The company operates across four primary business segments–Architecture and Network Solutions, Safety and Motion, Autonomous and Commercial Mobility, and User Experience–serving global automotive OEMs with products spanning advanced driver assistance systems, display solutions, braking systems, and connectivity hardware.
