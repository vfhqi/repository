If sector re-rating were primary, the move would be more synchronised. The divergence between NOS (spiking) and TIT (drifting down through March) in the same two-week window is the clearest evidence that the cause is company-specific.

Unexplained residual: The persistence of the grind above €5.20 through May (post-dividend, post-Q1 results) suggests there may be a strategic narrative component — possible M&A optionality or further enterprise deal pipeline — that has not yet surfaced in public disclosures.
