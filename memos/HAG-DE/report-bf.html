Section 3 – Industry Structure + Concentration + Value Chain Economics 49 findings

Section summary
  1. unprecedented global defense spending and European modernization initiatives are driving a structural supercycle in high-value defense electronics.
  2. the European market remains highly fragmented along national lines, resulting in duplicative systems as nations prioritize technological sovereignty.
  3. competitive rivalry is moderated by entrenched co-dependence between established Tier 1 primes and sovereign military procurement agencies.
  4. Germany's defense spending acts as a massive gravity force with the €100 billion special fund securing long-term backlog visibility.
  5. technical success demands decades of specialized IP in sensor technology and MMICs, creating nonfinancial barriers that block new entrants.
