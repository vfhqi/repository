  • Positive: LMWH raw material (heparin API) costs declining, partially offsetting revenue price decline

  • Negative: Heparin price erosion compressing gross margin on that segment

  • CDMO transition — Moderna unwinding:
  • Moderna fill-and-finish peak: FY2021-22 (specific figures not in public data).

Sceptical bullet: What's the bearish read on strategic execution? ROVI has simultaneously: (a) acquired a manufacturing facility (Phoenix) requiring integration, (b) delayed a CDMO contract (prefilled syringe), (c) expanded headcount and SG&A faster than revenue, (d) accelerated R&D to the upper bound of guidance. Management bandwidth at a mid-cap pharma/CDMO is finite — the Phoenix acquisition, Okedi US launch, ISM Phase III, and CDMO contract transition are all consuming management attention simultaneously.
