The Gym Group plc: Report on UK Budget Fitness Leader
Key Takeaways

High-Value, Low-Price Model : The Gym Group operates a distinctive UK-focused fitness model centered on 24/7 access, contract-free memberships, and quality equipment at affordable monthly price points, executing its "Next Chapter" strategy to strengthen core estates and accelerate new site rollouts.

Labor-Light Competitive Advantage : The company employs only two full-time staff per gym, creating profound insulation against UK wage inflation including National Living Wage increases and National Insurance contribution hikes.

Strong Post-Pandemic Recovery : Revenue reached £244.9m in FY 2025 with Group Adjusted EBITDA Less Normalised Rent growing 19% to £56.7m, while generating robust free cash flow of £38.3m enabling self-funded expansion and a £10m share buyback program.

Exceptional Return Profile : Management rigorously targets 30% Return on Invested Capital on mature sites, successfully expanding from 21% in 2023 to 27% in 2025, with underlying performance effectively hitting the 30% target when excluding 13 legacy workforce-dependent gyms.

Structural Demand Tailwinds : UK gym penetration reached a record 16.6% in 2025, with low-cost gyms capturing market share from mid-market operators while Gen Z consumers (44% of membership base) view fitness as essential spending, amplified by GLP-1 weight-loss medications driving strength training demand.

Defensible UK Duopoly but Limited Scale : The Gym Group holds a strong position alongside PureGym in the UK budget fitness market with structural cost advantages over premium operators, though it lacks the geographical diversification and procurement power of continental peer Basic-Fit's 1,600+ club footprint.

High Forward Visibility : The company provides crystal-clear multi-year guidance targeting 75 new sites over three years with leverage maintained below 2.0x, supported by electricity costs fixed through October 2027, though execution risks remain around the aggressive rollout pace and ongoing IT system overhaul.
