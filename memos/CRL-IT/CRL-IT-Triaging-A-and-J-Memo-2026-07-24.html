B.1 Business Description 8 findings
Business Description -- A plain-English description of what the company does, how it makes money, and its primary market.
1.
HVAC/R controls specialist. Carel designs electronic controllers, sensors, expansion valves, and humidification systems for heating, ventilation, air-conditioning, and refrigeration — serving OEMs, contractors, and installers across 12 global production sites.
2.
Revenue scale. €629m FY2025 (+10.6% organic CER); Q1 2026 at €170.9m (+19.7% organic) — fourth consecutive quarter of double-digit organic growth.
3.
Margin profile. EBITDA 19.7% FY2025 → 21.5% Q1 2026; gross margins ~50%; EBIT ~16–17%; net profit €73.6m FY2025 (+17.6%). First net-cash positive (€18.4m excl. IFRS 16 = €48.3m) in company history.
4.
Asset-light controls layer. CapEx only 3.6% of revenues FY2025 (peak 5.5% in FY2024 for plant expansion); ROI 15.4% FY2025 vs 11.5% FY2024 — improving but not yet Belimo-level (Belimo ROCE ~36%).
5.
Channel. 66% OEM, 20% installers/contractors, 14% distributors. No single customer >5% of revenue; top 40 customers ~50% of revenue. Strong but not exceptional diversification.
6.
Geography. EMEA 64%, North America 20% (fastest growth), APAC 14%, South America 2%.
7.
Investment style. Structural growth compounder with cyclical overlay (heat pump destocking created 2024 entry point); moving into a multi-year EPS upgrade cycle now the destocking has resolved.
8.
M&A. Kiona (82.4%, €164.8m, 2023) for SaaS/IoT platform — EBITDA ~30%, but international deployment slower than planned. SENVA ($34m) for North American sensors. Sauber (full buyout July 2025, €1.6m residual) for field services.
