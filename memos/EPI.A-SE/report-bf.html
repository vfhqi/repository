EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (BLUF)

Business Quality and Structural Competitive Position

Epiroc generates 80% of revenues through direct sales channels , bypassing distributors to establish deep customer relationships and rapid feedback loops that guide product development . The asset-light assembly model sources 75% of equipment product costs externally, enabling flexible cost structures and rapid production

scaling during cyclical downturns while maintaining capital expenditure at just 2-3% of revenues . Aftermarket services generate premium typical EBIT margins of 25-35% , representing margins 2-3x higher than original equipment sales , as harsh underground environments accelerate component wear and miners prioritize uptime . The customer base is highly diversified across approximately 150 nations globally, with the top ten customers accounting for only 18% of total sales , protecting against concentrated buyer dependency . Initial greenfield equipment selection locks customers into life-of-mine fleet standardization, as switching brands imposes prohibitive inventory costs and downtime penalties exceeding USD 130,000 per hour for critical assets . 7
