  • ❌ Adjustments trend: Adjusted EBITDA excludes restructuring, integration costs, and equity compensation — each of which is recurring in a transformation scenario; cumulative adjustments over FY2023–H1 2025 total approximately €75–90m [#A]

  • NWC trend: Working capital movements have been a modest headwind in each period (~€20–40m outflow per half-year) consistent with transformation spending; no evidence of aggressive receivables management or payables extension to flatter FCF [#A]
