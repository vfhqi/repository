Industry Concentration and Competitive Landscape

The top 45 European merchant acquirers processed an aggregate volume of $6 trillion in transactions in proforma 2023 , with the top 10 merchant acquirers capturing two-thirds of the total transaction volume . Worldpay led the peer set with 11% of total European Visa/Mastercard volume in pro-forma 2024, Adyen captured 10% , and Nexi captured 8% . Nexi commands an impressive 60% to 70% card payment market share within its highly under-penetrated Italian home market , but high-growth digital native competitors like Adyen continue to win market share using unified global platforms . Aggressive pricing from micro-merchant challengers like SumUp and Dojo squeeze legacy bank-led acquirers in regional SME markets , while competitive pressure from enterprise segment players means Adyen and Stripe now represent approximately 50% of European web-based volumes . 25 27

Long-Term Disruption and Paradigm Positioning

Account-to-account payments represent formidable online disintermediation risks, capturing over 20% of volumes in Poland, though ownership of Przelewwy24 allows Nexi to natively capture high-growth A2A flows . The European Payments Initiative digital wallet leverages real-time SEPA transfers, with retail e-commerce acceptance live in Germany in late 2025, and Nexi acts as an official Wero acquirer . European software-integrated payment penetration remains low, with Italy at less than 5% compared to 50-60% in America, while over 90% of European software vendors target a single market . Nexi's investment thesis is shifting from a premium secular growth story to a high-yield, defensive utility cash-generator, with sustainable long-term growth secured by persistent cash-heavy pools in Italy where card penetration is 38% .
