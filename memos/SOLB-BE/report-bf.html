EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (BLUF)

Solvay operates a simplified, cost-focused essential chemicals model following its December 2023 demerger from Syensqo, positioning itself purely as an essential chemical supplier across soda ash, bicarbonate, hydrogen peroxide, silica, rare earths, and fluorine . The company commands dominant global market positions , holding the number-two capacity position globally ex-China at 19% in soda ash and the global number-one position in sodium bicarbonate at 16% market share , alongside the global number-one capacity spot in hydrogen peroxide . Solvay captures high downstream margins through proprietary highly dispersible silica (HDS) technology with 30% global market share and pipeline-integrated peroxides under long-term, high-margin, take-orpay contracts .

The company operates within highly concentrated oligopolistic markets where structural forces create both opportunities and risks. Ex-China soda ash concentration remains high with the top five players holding 70%
