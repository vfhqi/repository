BMS accounts for the majority of Nanobiotix's forward cash commitments; a partnership read-across would move the stock identically to a data miss. The piece of evidence that would flip the call from "data disappointment" to "BMS-signal" is any BMS quarterly earnings comment, pipeline update, or SEC filing that references NBTXR3 scope changes around the same window.

What remains unexplained
