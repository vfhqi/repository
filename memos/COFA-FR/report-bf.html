Section summary
  1. Coface operates a highly resilient, diversified multi-channel model where transactional insurance cyclicality is offset by high-margin service and business information growth.
  2. the company delivers highly resilient through-the-cycle return profiles, driven by exceptional underwriting discipline and a robust 197% Solvency II ratio.

Coface operates a highly resilient trade credit insurance franchise , serving approximately 100,000 global clients while maintaining the third position in a consolidated triopoly structure that controls 60% of worldwide market share . The company defends a stable 14% global market share against larger rivals Allianz Trade (27%) and Atradius (19%) , leveraging proprietary database assets covering 245 million companies to generate high-margin business information revenues that grew 16.2% organically during 2025 . Technical underwriting quality consistently outperforms peers by over 8 percentage points on combined ratio metrics , while robust capital positioning at 197% Solvency II ratio–well above the 155-175% comfort range–supports exceptional shareholder distributions . Structural competitive moats stem from decades of accumulated payment history data that remain virtually unreplicable by fintech entrants , though persistent pricing erosion averaging negative 1.1% to 1.6% annually reflects ongoing competitive intensity across mature European markets .
