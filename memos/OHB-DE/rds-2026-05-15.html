Why the move is concentrated post-Q1, not at cartel clearance: the cartel clearance was a necessary condition; the Q1 earnings call likely provided the sufficient condition — management confidence on the SATCOMBw bid timeline and the strength of the backlog-to-revenue conversion rate. A 45% backlog increase to €3.35bn on revenues of €200.8m implies a coverage ratio of over 4x, which is unusually strong for a space contractor.
