EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (BLUF) 28 findings

VAT Group AG operates as the undisputed global leader in high-precision vacuum valves for semiconductor manufacturing, commanding a quasi-monopolistic market position that translates into exceptional financial returns and structural pricing power. The company's business foundation rests on irreplaceable technical expertise, decades-long customer specification lock-ins, and massive R&D reinvestment advantages that create formidable barriers to competitive entry. VAT holds approximately 71% overall market share in semiconductor vacuum valves, rising to over 90% in leading-edge applications, while consistently delivering gross margins above 60% and return on invested capital exceeding 35%. Despite severe cyclicality inherent in wafer fab equipment spending and concentrated customer exposure to major OEMs, VAT's operational flexibility, proactive capacity expansions, and expanding adjacent product portfolio position the group to structurally outgrow baseline WFE markets through 2029. Management targets low-to-mid teens revenue CAGR based on normalized 2029 WFE spend of USD 150 billion, with EBITDA margins sustained between 30% and 37% and free cash flow conversion of 60% to 70% of EBITDA.
