Executive Summary -- We are looking for a clear, conviction-carrying statement of the investment thesis, key ratings, and actionable conclusion; avoiding vague or overly hedged language.
Judgement summary
Bottom Line Up Front: Elisa parks at Triaging on a broken delivery-congruency test and a weak setup-fit read, against a genuinely cheap headline multiple.
  • Overall verdict. D Elisa (ELISA-FI) presents a cost-cutting defender pattern, not a change-driven upgrader: the change forces are real but internally-generated and margin-only, while the top line has turned negative for the first time.
  • Key risk. Mobile service revenue contracted 0.1% year-over-year in Q1 2026, the first negative print in recent memory, against a bottom-quartile peer rank versus Tele2 (TEL2.B-SE) and Telia (TELIA-SE).

The single most important takeaway: a cheap multiple is not, on its own, a change-driven case.
