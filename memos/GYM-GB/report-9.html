Execution Risks: CapEx Catch-Ups and Operational Gearing

The strategic decision to accelerate the site rollout to approximately 75 new locations over three years introduces
signiﬁcant operational gearing and execution risk. [84] [82] Rapidly expanding the estate inherently increases ﬁxed

actively addressing this vulnerability, having purposefully stepped up property maintenance capital expenditure to
