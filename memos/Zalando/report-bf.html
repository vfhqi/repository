EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (BLUF)

Zalando is transitioning from a transactional wholesaler into a multi-layered ecosystem spanning marketplace, logistics, and advertising services, with partner business GMV share increasing to 36.6% in Q1 2026 . This structural pivot fundamentally reshapes the group's competitive position within European fashion retail, moving profit pools away from low-margin inventory-led distribution toward high-margin retail media revenues that grew 42% in FY25 to reach 1.8% of group GMV . The B2C segment includes the flagship Zalando app, Lounge by Zalando, and the newly consolidated ABOUT YOU commerce business, with each app targeting distinct customer shopping occasions while sharing common foundational tech and data infrastructure . The B2B operating system offers modular logistics via ZEOS Smart Logistics, which provides partners a 25% cost advantage over traditional drop-shipping, and Tradebyte serves as the integration layer connecting over 1,000 brands to 90+ European marketplaces .
