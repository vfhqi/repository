EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (BLUF)

Fineco Bank operates a vertically integrated, three-pillar business model combining digital banking, online brokerage, and wealth management advisory services , generating highly diversified revenues across banking transactional deposits, investing asset management fees, and brokerage commissions. The bank serves over 1.80 million Italian clients as of early 2026 , leveraging a hybrid distribution model that pairs proprietary digital platforms with a nationwide network of 3,076 Personal Financial Advisors managing approximately 90% of platform assets . This integrated one-stop-solution architecture creates extremely high customer switching barriers by consolidating daily banking transactions, long-term investment portfolios, and global brokerage execution within a single current account. 1 2
