Bearish interpretation (Margin Compression via Competition) (cont.): If OEM is forced to overpay for lower-quality, lower-margin distribution assets merely to satisfy EQT's aggressive top-line growth mandates, the group's consolidated ROCE and EBITA margins will face severe, multi-year compression.
