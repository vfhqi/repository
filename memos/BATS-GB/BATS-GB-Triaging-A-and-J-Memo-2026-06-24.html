B - Executive Summary 51 findings
Executive Summary -- We are looking for a clear, conviction-carrying statement of the investment thesis, key ratings, and actionable conclusion; avoiding vague or overly hedged language.
Judgement summary
BLUF. BAT is a GBP25.6bn global tobacco oligopolist undergoing a credible but incomplete transformation to New Generation Products (NGP). The Velo pouch business in the US is the single most important change force: 249% volume growth, 18% national share, and three times the gross profit per unit versus combustibles. Against this, change story faces structural drags: E7 fails on case complexity (too many simultaneous moving parts); -E7 D is the principal risk to progression; ROIC at 9% disqualifies the high-quality compounder setup; and illicit trade capturing 70-85% of US vapour market undermines Vuse economics materially. Primary setup is RA5 (huge change-from-current in solid quality), with RA3 (corporate change-driven) as secondary. A 5,204p swing high stands 17% above current price; the stock has been below that level for 1,311 days. Recommendation: progress to ESA at low/medium conviction.
