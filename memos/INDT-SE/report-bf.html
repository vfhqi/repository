EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (BLUF) 35 findings

Indutrade operates a highly decentralised technical-trading and niche manufacturing business model across 223 independent companies, delivering resilient through-cycle margins insulated by extensive segment and geographic diversification with no single customer exceeding 3% of sales. The Group generated SEK 32.2bn in net sales during 2025, positioning it as the largest Nordic serial compounder by revenue scale, though its 9% three-year sales CAGR trails Addtech's 11% and its 15.3% EBITDA margin sits substantially below Lifco's peer- leading 24.9%. Indutrade has delivered an impressive 15% EPS CAGR since its 2005 IPO and maintains exceptional cash conversion with net income translating to lease-adjusted FCFE at a median of 102% over the past decade. The business occupies a highly profitable technical trading and niche manufacturing niche within the industrial value chain, where profit pools accrue disproportionately to specialised niches and proprietary product owners, defended by secure pricing power and high technical expertise. Substantial customer moats through proprietary technical specifications and the mechanical momentum of a self-funding acquisition flywheel enable predictable compounding, with capital-light cash flows from existing units continually funding 15- 20 annual deals without equity dilution. 1
