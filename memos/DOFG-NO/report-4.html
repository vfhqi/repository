  • Tidewater's scale gives it negotiating leverage on global contracts that smaller peers (including DOF) cannot match

  • DOF competes primarily on subsea specialisation and relationships with Brazilian and North Sea operators

  • However, if oil prices decline or E&P spending disappoints, the cyclical component unwinding could overshadow the structural supply constraint
