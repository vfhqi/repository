EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (BLUF)

Givaudan is undergoing its most significant leadership transition in two decades, with Gilles Andrier transitioning from CEO to Chairman on March 19, 2026, and Christian Stammkoetter taking over as Chief Executive Officer effective March 1, 2026, marking a surprising departure from internal succession tradition . The company balances this unprecedented external CEO hire with deep industrial biotech and CPG expertise while retaining a best-in-class ethical and collaborative culture that outclasses regional competitors in talent attraction . The external hire surprised the financial community, as Stammkoetter brings over 30 years of consumer packaged goods experience from Danone, though analysts note the combination of CEO transition and upgraded organic growth targets will keep investors cautious near term .
