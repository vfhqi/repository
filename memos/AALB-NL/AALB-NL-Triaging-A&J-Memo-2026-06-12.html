B.1 Cover context
Business Description -- A plain-English description of what the company does, how it makes money, and its primary market.

Aalberts Industries NV (AALB-AMS) is a Dutch precision industrial with EUR 3.1bn revenue across three segments: Semicon (precision fluid delivery for semiconductor equipment, 15% revenue, ~21% EBITA), Industry (aerospace, medical, high-tech industrial components, ~35% revenue), and Building Technology (hydronic flow control for commercial and residential HVAC, ~50% revenue). The company operates on a local-for-local manufacturing model (100+ sites, 30+ countries), has a 45-year history as a serial acquirer, and is currently executing the Thrive 2030 portfolio transformation under CEO Simonetta — the first external non-insider CEO appointment in company history.
