KD2: TECHNOLOGY RISK — AIR COOLING VS LIQUID COOLING AT 200W+ GPU TDP #J

Rating: YELLOW

  • The technology transition is underway and Munters is responding — but this is an active competitive dynamic, not a resolved risk. At 200W+ GPU TDP (Blackwell at 700W+ per GPU; GB200 NVL72 racks at 100kW+), pure air cooling is insufficient for the densest AI factory configurations. The industry is moving toward hybrid architectures: liquid cooling to the server level combined with air cooling for the residual thermal envelope. #J

  • Munters' two-pronged response is credible but partnership-dependent. First, the LCX CDU family (2025) addresses direct-to-chip liquid cooling. The BSEK 2.0 post-Q1 order explicitly includes both high-capacity CDUs and over-rack CRAHs — a hybrid architecture win, not pure air cooling. Second, the ZutaCore partnership (September 2024) integrates ZutaCore's Hypercool chip-level solution with Munters' SyCool thermosiphon loop — targeting waterless operation and PUE 1.05-1.07. #A

  • [AS·Expert Call]: ZutaCore partnership addresses the direct-to-chip liquid cooling requirement where Munters historically had no organic presence. The combined system provides primary and secondary loop solutions for high-density AI configurations. #I

  • Munters' structural hedge: a hybrid architecture world — the most likely near-term outcome — favours Munters more than a pure liquid cooling world. In hybrid deployments, CDUs handle chip-level heat but residual heat still requires air-side management and chiller plant capacity. Munters owns both sides in a hybrid. #A

  • [AS·Expert Call]: The liquid cooling market is highly competitive and challenging for new entrants due to supply chain constraints and dominance of large, vertically integrated players. Munters relies on partnerships for direct-to-chip capabilities rather than organic IP. #I

  • ❌ The industry's pace of GPU density escalation is faster than 2023-vintage forecasts anticipated. NVIDIA's roadmap points to continued power density increases. If the industry reaches a tipping point where colocators mandate liquid cooling as a minimum specification, Munters' air-side installed base becomes a liability. Munters is entering liquid cooling from a follower position, not leadership. The ZutaCore dependency introduces third-party relationship duration risk. #J
