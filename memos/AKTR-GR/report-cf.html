EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (BLUF)

Net Signal on Materiality of Change

The strategic pivot represents highly material change across leadership, capital structure, and business model. CEO Alexandros Exarchou assumed leadership to drive the transition from Intrakat heritage, orchestrating a corporate transformation launched in 2024 that restructured the parent into a pure holding company. Management deployed approximately €282 million in 2025 across five closed acquisitions including the pivotal €252 million purchase of Aktor Concessions and smaller bolt-on deals targeting facility management, renewables, and network construction capabilities. The shareholder base was fundamentally reset when Winex Investments acquired a 34.78% stake in July 2022, with strategic partners Castellano Properties and Blue Silk combining to hold over 64% of capital and enabling aggressive capital raises to fund the diversification strategy. The organizational structure was radically simplified with the parent operating as a pure holding company providing administrative services while construction activities were hived down into Aktor Constructions subsidiary and concessions spin-off into Aktor Participations Concessions-PPP. 5
