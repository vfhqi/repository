2. Quarterly Progression & Segment Delivery

The year played out in two halves: strong Q1-Q3 with double-digit organic growth and quarterly EBITDA records, then a planned Q4 step-down from Champlain exit. Service & Accessories was the breakout segment.

  • Judgement: The quarterly cadence tells a clear story — peak performance Q2-Q3 (margin high-water mark 16.4% in Q3) followed by a predictable Q4 fade. The lumpiness is inherent to project-based HV cable businesses. No surprises here. #J [C]
  • Judgement: Service & Accessories is the standout segment: +22% organic growth, margin nearly doubled from 9.7% to 19.1%, Q4 organic growth +31%. If this is structural (supply-constrained pricing, offshore repair demand), it changes the group margin trajectory materially. #J [C+AS]
