  • Overall operator + governance verdict: Medium plausibility — credible transition, structurally clean governance, but the new CEO's operational depth in tower infrastructure remains unproven at scale and the Criteria/board concentration is a latent governance overhang

Investor communication:
- Capital Markets Day (2022, 2024): Aisa Mancho presents the financial framework in detail; quantitatively rigorous; willing to walk through bridge from GAAP to RLFCF in public; not evasive on debt maturity schedule
