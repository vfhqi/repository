EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (BLUF) 27 findings

Vitrolife operates a highly predictable, end-to-end IVF equipment and services portfolio with 91% recurring revenues across three business segments: Consumables (advanced culture media and disposables), Technologies (EmbryoScope time-lapse systems and eWitness software), and Genetics Services (preimplantation genetic testing under the Igenomix brand), serving approximately 75% of global fertility clinics through direct commercial teams in major markets and distributors in emerging regions . The group generated SEK 3,440M in FY2025 with balanced geographic exposure across EMEA (38% of sales), Americas (33%), and Asia-Pacific (29%) , though reported revenues declined 5% in SEK terms due to severe currency headwinds despite delivering solid 4% organic growth in local currencies .
