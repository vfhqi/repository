Activist Presence and Governance Dynamics

No active activist presence currently exists in AS library for Flatex DeGiro SE following resolution of historical shareholder conflicts. Successfully resolved historical governance friction as Supervisory Board refresh settled public disputes raised by largest shareholder GfBk with Martin Korbmacher stepping down as Chairman in March 2025. Board composition strengthened through insider transition as Stefan Müller assumed Chairman role in 2025 after serving as Deputy since 2017 while founder Bernd Förtsch holding 18% stake joined Supervisory Board and independent directors Hans-Hermann Lotter and Martina Pfeifer added in June 2025 to boost competence. Compensation reforms implemented addressing investor grievances including reduced executive paycaps from EUR 15m to EUR 7.5m for CEO and from EUR 12m to EUR 5.5m for other Management Board members plus new Share Ownership Guidelines requiring board members to hold 1x fixed salary in shares and improved remuneration transparency. Residual governance risk remains as concentrated shareholder representation by Bernd Förtsch on board could amplify conflicts of interest or destabilize management continuity if strategies diverge.
