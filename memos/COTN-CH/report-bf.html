EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (BLUF) 20 findings

COMET Holding AG operates as a specialized supplier of radio frequency plasma control subsystems and industrial X-ray technologies, serving the global semiconductor equipment and advanced packaging inspection markets.

The company operates three core divisions leveraging specialized radio frequency and industrial X-ray technologies: Plasma Control Technologies (manufacturing vacuum capacitors, RF generators, and RF impedance matching networks), X-Ray Systems (developing standardized computed tomography solutions for nondestructive advanced packaging inspection), and Industrial X-Ray Modules (providing compact stationary and portable X-ray sources for industrial non-destructive material testing) .

The business exhibits extreme cyclical volatility tied to memory capital expenditure cycles, yet maintains formidable structural moats in vacuum capacitor technology and promising secular tailwinds from AI-driven semiconductor capacity expansions and advanced packaging adoption.

Near-term financial returns remain cyclically depressed due to the strategic Penang capacity expansion and prolonged memory downturn, while long-term value creation hinges on successful commercialization of the Synertia RF generator platform and the CA20 advanced packaging inspection system.
