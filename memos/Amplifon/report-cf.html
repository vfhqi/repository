EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (BLUF)

Amplifon faces no active activist presence, but the company is executing a transformative strategic pivot that fundamentally reshapes its competitive positioning and financial profile over the next three to five years. The EUR 2.3bn acquisition of GN Hearing represents a structural shift from pure-play retail to vertical integration spanning manufacturing and R&D, introducing both substantial cost synergy opportunities and meaningful execution risks that will define the investment case through fiscal year 2029.
