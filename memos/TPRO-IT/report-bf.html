Technoprobe commands a dominant position in logic testing through vertical MEMS probe cards , operating as device-specific consumables tied to wafer volumes that isolate the model from near-term capital expenditure volatility while strategic equity partnerships with Teradyne and Advantest cement its position as the preferred test interface developer . The company achieves superior average gross margins near 45.0% through highvolume vertical integration, with significant volume expansion driving rapid profitability improvements demonstrated by the 37.0% EBITDA margin achieved in Q1 2026 . Proprietary MEMS platform technology acquired through Microfabrica in 2019 now drives over 90% of revenues , establishing an unreplicable manufacturing monopoly . Consolidated revenues reached a record €628.4 million in FY2025 , representing strong 15.7% year-on-year growth driven by artificial intelligence demand . 1
