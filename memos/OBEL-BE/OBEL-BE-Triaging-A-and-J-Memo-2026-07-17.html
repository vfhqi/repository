1.1

4.
Competitive position: Number-two or number-three player in Belgian mobile (behind Proximus and contested with Telenet/BASE); owns the VOO cable network in Wallonia; structurally disadvantaged in Flanders where it pays regulated cable wholesale rates to Telenet
5.
Financial character: Revenue-weak, margin-expanding through integration savings; zero dividend (FY24 and FY25 eliminated); capex-heavy cycle nearing peak
5.1
Earnings per share depression: Consensus -91% EPS contraction 2026F vs +5% sector median, driven by depreciation and interest on VOO acquisition debt

6.1
Free float constraint: Orange S.A. at 74.7% ownership; public free float estimated at ~15%: highly illiquid for a listed equity; Orange S.A. has stated no intention to re-launch a tender offer
7.
