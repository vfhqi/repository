BRAV #5 — LTM Earnings Delivery [C]

BRAVIDA HOLDING AB — LTM EARNINGS DELIVERY ANALYSIS

1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

- Overall verdict: Mixed delivery. BRAV missed full-year 2024 EPS estimates by 1.5% despite revenue tracking in line with expectations. The earnings miss was driven by operational challenges (Denmark write-downs, bad debts/bankruptcies) rather than fundamental demand collapse.

- Direction of estimate revisions: Revenue essentially flat YoY (SEK 29,653M vs SEK 29,423M, +0.78%), but EBIT margin contracted 70bps from 5.9% to 5.2% on an apples-to-apples basis, reflecting execution headwinds and one-off charges. Profit before tax margin fell from 4.2% (FY2023) to 3.6% (FY2024).

  • Management confidence: Cautiously optimistic. Guidance for 2025 projects EBITA margin near 5%, acknowledging market has "stopped declining" but demand recovery will be gradual. Management tone on outlook is measured, not enthusiastic. Emphasis on "margin over volume" strategy and continued cost discipline.

- Key surprises in LTM: Organic growth negative or near-zero in 3 of 4 quarters (Q1: -6%, Q2: +1%, Q3: -3%, Q4: ~0%). Order backlog contracted from SEK 17.8bn (Q1) to SEK 14.9bn (year-end), down 16%. Bad debts and project write-downs material detractors. Denmark operation required new management and restructuring. However, cash conversion remained strong at 105-134% quarterly, and service revenue grew +8% in Q3.

- One-off items significant: Items affecting comparability material in Q4 (margin ex-IAC: 8.3% vs reported 7.5%, indicating SEK 60M+ of non-recurring items). Full-year impact of restructuring in Denmark/southern Sweden partially masked underlying operational trends.

- Earnings delivery quality verdict: ORANGE. Revenue in line but EPS miss. Underlying organic growth weak and margin delivery below 2023. Profitability execution hampered by geographic-specific operational issues (fixable) but also genuine market weakness (slower-to-fix). Not a red flag (company still cash-generative, balance sheet intact), but yellow on operational momentum.

Volume likely down 3-5% FY2024 on weak installation demand.

Company explicitly notes strict project selection (turning down low-margin work), which is disciplined but signals they are not winning market share.

If this discipline yields margin improvement in 2025-26, the 2024 organic miss becomes a buying signal. If 2025 organic remains negative and margins don't expand, it's a warning sign of structural market share loss.

- Acquisition-driven growth offsetting organic weakness: Ten acquisitions added SEK 580M (~2% of revenue). Without M&A, FY2024 revenue would have been down 1-2%. This is below-trend for Bravida historically.

- Organic growth quality rating: RED. Negative in 3 of 4 quarters, mix deterioration, no visible inflection despite sequential improvement in Q4. Without M&A, BRAV would have reported declining revenue.

OVERALL EARNINGS DELIVERY ASSESSMENT

VERDICT: ORANGE (Cautious)

Bravida missed FY2024 EPS estimates by 1.5% and reported flat revenue with margin compression (-70bps reported, -10bps ex-one-offs). Organic growth was negative for 3 of 4 quarters. Order backlog contracted 16% YTD. However, balance sheet remains strong (1.2-1.5x net debt/EBITDA, 105%+ cash conversion), and management's "margin over volume" strategy is disciplined.

Forward guidance for 2025 is sparse and cautious ("EBITA margin close to 5%"). Peer comparison shows Bravida is mid-table: clearly outperformed by Caverion but outperforming Instalco. Risks are tilted to downside, but upside achievable if Nordic macro stabilizes.

Key inflection point: 2025 order intake. If intake accelerates, backlog stabilizes, and 2025 becomes a stabilization year. If intake remains flat or declines, 2025 becomes a decline year. Will be visible in Q1 2025 results.

For the triaging investor: BRAV is a candidate for "trough earnings with margin recovery" thesis, contingent on inflection in order intake. Not a disqualifier, but not a compelling setup until order intake shows inflection and FY2025 guidance is upgraded from "close to 5%" to "5.0-5.5%".
