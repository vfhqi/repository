Fee blending pressure from LP co-investment demands : Institutional investors increasingly utilize co- investment schemes to blend down overall fee expenses, with large LPs securing side-letter rights demanding substantial co-investment allocations alongside primary fund commitments , creating structural margin compression if co-investment ratios exceed sustainable thresholds .
