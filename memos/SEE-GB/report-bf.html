EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (BLUF) 25 findings

Seeing Machines designs AI-powered operator monitoring systems measuring driver gaze and cognitive state, generating highly scalable, high-margin automotive royalty income through OEM, Aftermarket, and Future Mobility divisions. Statutory revenue grew from US$39.00M in FY2022 to US$62.34M in FY2025, with high-margin automotive royalties increasing 35% year-over-year to US$14.40M, though persistent operating losses continued with a net loss of US$25.27M reported in FY2025. The global driver monitoring market is projected to expand from US$2.50bn in 2025 to US$19.80bn by 2032, with total DMS penetration estimated to surge from under one million units annually to 22 million by 2026. The high-barrier automotive DMS market operates as a tight duopoly between two primary players, with Seeing Machines maintaining a leading position holding over 50% share of current production volumes.
