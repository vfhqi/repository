§1 Executive Summary

Liberty Global's Q1-26 results represent a genuine but partial inflection point. The group reported its highest quarterly revenue growth rate (+8.8% YoY) and strongest EBITDA margin expansion (+12.9%) in several years, yet this headline improvement is partly a function of reported-currency translation and Telenet's normalisation after the Belgian football rights gap distorted prior comps. The more durable signal is operational: Telenet's Q1-26 broadband net adds were the best in a decade, and management reiterated all 2026 guidance metrics — a credible hold given Q1 trajectory. The VodafoneZiggo full-consolidation transaction announced simultaneously (February 2026) transforms the strategic picture and introduces a Euronext-listed Ziggo Group entity as the medium-term value-unlock vehicle.
