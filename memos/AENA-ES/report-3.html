The stock bottomed and spent most of 2020–2021 in Stage 1 accumulation, trading €10–€15 with a flat-to-gently-rising 200DMA. Volume contracted during the base — classic accumulation behaviour. Stage 2 advance (2023–Feb 2026). The stock advanced from ~€15 (Jan-23) to €28.86 (Feb-26) — a 92% gain over ~3 years. The advance was orderly: higher highs, higher lows, price consistently above a rising 200DMA. Key staging posts: €18–19 year-end 2023, €23 year-end 2024, €28.86 ATH in Feb-26. The 200DMA rose for 12 consecutive months (through Apr-26 data). No Minervini violations were recorded until the current sell-off.
