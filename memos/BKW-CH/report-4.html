§9 — Sceptical Synthesis (Cross-Cutting)

  • Verdict: The strongest bearish case is a dual compression event: Swiss regulatory WACC reset compresses Netz returns, while Engineering/Services M&A disappoints on integration, degrading the two pillars that supported FY2024 outperformance simultaneously [#J]

  • Reverse-engineered bear: The positive thesis rests on: (1) Netz RAB growth + stable regulated returns; (2) Engineering/Services compounding via M&A; (3) management conservatism enabling beats [#A]

  • Sub: Each of these has a specific breaking point: (1) Swiss ElCom WACC reset; (2) acquisition integration failure; (3) guidance miss on Energy segment surprise

  • Single largest risk: The Swiss ElCom regulatory review (next tariff period ~2025-2027) is the highest-conviction single risk; if WACC is reduced in line with falling Swiss risk-free rates (10Y Swiss government bond ~1.0-1.2%), the regulatory return on BKW's RAB could compress 50-100bps [#J]

  • Sub: A 50bps WACC compression on an estimated RAB of CHF ~2.5-3bn implies ~CHF 12-15m EBIT reduction — not catastrophic but directionally negative and potentially ongoing

  • Sub: This risk is structural, not recoverable through operational improvement, and would be invisible to an investor focused only on trailing growth metrics

  • Early warning — Netz: Watch for Swiss ElCom consultation documents on the 2025-2027 tariff period; any reduction in the permitted rate of return (Kapitalkosten) would be an early warning signal [#A]
