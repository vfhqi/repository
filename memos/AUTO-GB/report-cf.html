EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (BLUF) 6 findings

Autotrader Group PLC operates as the dominant UK used car marketplace with 70% operating margins and £600m projected capital returns in FY27 , but faces near-term headwinds from Deal Builder integration friction, retailer forecourt attrition , and stock dilution that triggered a 2% revenue miss in FY26. The platform retains formidable two-sided network effects commanding 6x more consumer minutes than all competitors combined, yet employee engagement collapsed from 91% to 72% following organizational restructuring and mandatory transaction tool adoption that sparked severe dealer backlash . Material regulatory risk has intensified as the CMA investigates whether one-star reviews were suppressed on Autotrader's platform, with potential fines up to 10% of global turnover under the Digital Markets, Competition and Consumers Act 2024. (GOV.UK)
