Q1 2026 financial delivery and consensus beat

Q1 net sales – minor miss: Nolato's Q1 sales of SEK 2,357m missed ABG Sundal Collier's Gilani's estimate of SEK 2,394m, primarily due to a severe 7% exchange rate headwind

Engineered Solutions margin – positive surprise: Engineered Solutions' EBITA margin rose to 10.6% from 10.1% in Q1 2025, driven by a favorable product mix with a higher contribution from Materials

Raw material cost inflation and pass-through lag

9.
Forecast adjustments – broker response: DNB Carnegie's Laséen reduced 2026e EBITA by 4% to incorporate the raw material price lag into Q2 and Q3 models
10.
Segment exposure – medical plastics: Plastic input costs account for 20-25% of the Medical Solutions segment's selling price, representing a significant exposure to polymer price volatility
