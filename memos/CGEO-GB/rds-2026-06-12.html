Feb 2026 +11% spike: The FY2025 results (24 Feb) delivered a material positive surprise — 61.2% NAV growth — and a new $50m buyback launch combined to create a concentrated re-rating moment. The buyback announcement was the news-break catalyst; the results justified a higher NAV anchor.

Decision prompt for follow-on work: What is the current BGEO weight in CGEO NAV as of June 2026, given post-FTSE inclusion re-pricing?
