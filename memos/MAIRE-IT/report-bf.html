EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (BLUF) 21 findings

MAIRE operates a dual-engine business model combining high-volume, low-margin EPC construction with highmargin, asset-light technology licensing , creating a complementary risk-return profile that distinguishes it from traditional engineering contractors. The integrated model combines low-margin construction with high-margin technology licensing, boosting the overall competitive moat , while highly profitable technology licenses provide a resilient and capital-light stream with exceptionally high profit margins .

However, the business faces material concentration risks , with the $8.7 billion ADNOC Hail & Ghasha contract representing nearly 50% of the active backlog and the ten largest contracts accounting for 81.3% of consolidated revenues , exposing profitability to single-project execution delays or regional geopolitical disruptions in the Middle East. Ten major contracts accounted for 81.3% of consolidated revenues in the first half of 2025 , while the $8.7 billion ADNOC Hail & Ghasha award represents nearly 50% of the active backlog .
