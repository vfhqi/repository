EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (BLUF) 31 findings

Systemair operates a highly resilient, decentralised ventilation model balancing high-volume stock commodities with complex energy-efficient systems under new expansion-focused leadership. The company maintains formidable digital workflow lock-ins through proprietary BIM software integration, protecting margins from commodity competition while serving nearly 100,000 highly fragmented customer accounts across 51 countries. Recent financial performance demonstrates superior organic growth delivery relative to peers, though near-term margins reflect geographic mix headwinds and rising input costs. New CEO Robert Larsson has established an ambitious strategic mission to double group size in less than seven years, shifting focus from manufacturing expansion toward upgrading the commercial engine and accelerating programmatic M&A.
