EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (BLUF)

Overall execution confidence under the BRIGHT 2030 strategy is assessed at medium plausibility due to slow strategic evolution.

Insider Transactions and Capital Allocation Signals Executive directors completed zero conviction-driven open market purchases during the last 12 months, with all transactions limited to performance share vestings. The LTIP 2022-2024 series vested at only 49.7% of target, with CEO Rigaud receiving 11,592 shares worth €227,000. Rigaud holds 113,193 unvested conditional shares, providing substantial prospective long-term equity alignment. Management failed to deliver aggressive share buybacks at the Capital Markets Day, disappointing institutional capital return expectations. Former Givaudan colleagues indicate Rigaud needs to raise strategic urgency and note his consensus-driven approach slows necessary organizational restructurings.
