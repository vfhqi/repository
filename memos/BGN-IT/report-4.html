Net Inflow Trends and Asset Quality (2020–2025)

The Italian private banking industry saw a recovery in 2024 and 2025 following a sharp outflow period in 2023, with total industry net inflows reaching €22.98 billion in 2025. Banca Generali maintained a steady performance within this recovery, while peers showed significant divergence in volume and mix.

Annual Net Inflow Comparison

Banca Mediolanum: Tracked toward year-to-date inflows of €9.24 billion as of October 2025, with a strong focus on managed assets reaching €7.32 billion in that period.

Inflow Quality and Mix

Banca Generali’s inflow quality has shifted toward Assets under Investment , which accounted for 58% (€4.0 billion) of total 2025 inflows. By comparison, Mediolanum led the sector in managed asset bias, reaching record managed inflows of €7.60 billion in 2024 and guiding for €8.00–€8.50 billion in 2025.

Seasonality typically favors Q1; however, BGN noted an acceleration in 4Q25 as M&A-related headwinds (likely related to the Mediobanca/Generali situation) concluded.
