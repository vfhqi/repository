Strong balance sheet with rising leverage: The company holds approximately $20 billion in cash reserves against $31.86 billion in gross debt, though the net debt-to-capital ratio expanded to 22.7% by year-end 2025 from 17.3% in 2024, comparing favorably to Shell's 23.2% gearing but reflecting front-loaded wind capex and working capital volatility including $800-900 million in trading collateral requirements
