Customer Concentration and Switching Costs

Due to its retail advisor-led structure, Azimut's top ten wealth clients represent less than one percent of total wealth assets, with over one hundred thousand granular client relationships in Italy minimizing systemic concentration and single-source redemption risks, ensuring the loss of any single retail client family has negligible impact on consolidated recurring fee revenues.
