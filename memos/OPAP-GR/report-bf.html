Activist presence and governance structure

No active activist presence exists in the AlphaSense library for OPAP, with the KKCG Group set to control 78.4% of the combined Allwyn entity leaving a 21.6% free float following the March 2026 business combination. The refreshed eight-member board chaired by Karel Komarek ensures 50% independent non-executive directors improving oversight capabilities, while the consolidated CEO role under Jan Karas and retained local control for Greece and Cyprus operations provide strategic continuity. The governance transition introduces multinational regulatory exposure previously absent from OPAP as integration into Allwyn AG creates highly complex cross-border compliance requirements spanning European lottery markets, UK National Lottery operations, and new US daily fantasy sports exposure via PrizePicks.
