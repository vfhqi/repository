EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (BLUF)

Tate & Lyle has completed a decisive transformation into a focused specialty food ingredients leader, exiting bulk commodity processing through the Primient divestment and acquiring CP Kelco for US$1.8 billion to secure leadership in hydrocolloids, while entering a recommended all-cash takeover by Ingredion in June 2026 at a 59% premium . The business operates across three core specialty platforms– sweetening , mouthfeel , and fortification –serving global food and beverage manufacturers through a portfolio of over 250 texturants , proprietary sucralose , and soluble corn fibers , with mouthfeel representing 66% of pro forma sales and sweetening contributing 27% . The company holds global market leadership in pectin with a 46% segment share , and maintains high customer loyalty with two-thirds of the top 30 customers retained over five years due to formidable reformulation switching costs embedded in complex recipe systems . 1
