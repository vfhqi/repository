Section 5 – The possible investment case 11 findings

Section summary
  1. Brembo's forward investment case is strengthened by visible structural drivers including the industrial launch of Sensify and Euro 7 compliance, which together will significantly increase revenue content per vehicle.

Driver One – Sensify Software-Defined Platform

1.
Series production milestone: Sensify represents Brembo's intelligent, fluid-free braking platform that distributes software-defined intelligence at the wheel level, which entered series production in May 2026 for a leading global vehicle manufacturer
2.
Highly plausible adoption: Plausibility of Sensify fluid-free braking platform commercial success is high, as Brembo has already secured several contracts with multiple premium OEMs, each securing hundreds of thousands of vehicle volumes per year
3.
Margin dilutive threat: The primary risk of failure for Sensify is slower-than-expected software integration by vehicle manufacturers, which could delay high-margin software revenues and prolong costly industrial ramp-up phases

Driver Two – Euro 7 Regulation Compliance

4.
Coated disc opportunity: The new Euro 7 regulation sets strict limits on particulate emissions from brakes, creating a major commercial opportunity for Brembo's newly launched Greentell coated discs
5.
Strong technological readiness: Plausibility of Euro 7 volume wins is high, as Brembo's Greentell coating uses innovative Laser Metal Deposition technology to reduce PM10 emissions by up to 90.00% and disc wear by 80.00%
6.
Capital intensity failure: The mechanism of failure would be excessive capital intensity from expensive laser deposition machinery, which could depress free cash flow margins if volume commitments are not met

Driver Three – Footprint and Capacity Expansion

7.
Strategic local-for-local footprint: Brembo has expanded its industrial footprint in key low-cost regions, completing major factory expansions in Mexico, China, Poland, and Thailand to supply premium OEMs locally
8.
Plausible volume capture: Plausibility of capacity utilization is high, as the 50.00% capacity expansion at its carbon-ceramic joint venture with SGL Carbon is fully backed by surging luxury vehicle demand
9.
Compound bull requirements: For the bull case to deliver a 25.00%+ IRR, both the volume recovery of premium car production and the high-margin ramp of Sensify must occur concurrently, as these drivers are highly interdependent on OEM product cycles
10.
Other side of the story: If the global premium automotive market enters a severe downturn, Brembo's heavy capital expansion could lead to costly capacity underutilization and drag down the company's return on capital
