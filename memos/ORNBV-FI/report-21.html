§3 — Dimension 2: Customer Dynamics (SCAFFOLD)

  • Customer segmentation: map the customer base by segment (enterprise / SMB / consumer; public / private; domestic / international); revenue and gross margin profile per segment is the key data point
  • Switching costs: classify as high (contractual lock-in, embedded software, regulatory compliance requirements), medium (moderate retraining costs, moderate data portability friction), or low (commodity-like, price-driven)
  • Net Revenue Retention : for SaaS or subscription-adjacent models, NRR > 110% is the threshold for compounding customer economics; below 100% is a structural red flag at DD stage

  • Competitive set definition: establish who the relevant competitors are — direct (same product, same customer), adjacent (different product, same customer), and potential (different product, different customer, but could enter)
