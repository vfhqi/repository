EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (BLUF)

Gimv presents a compelling transformation story anchored by decisive strategic acceleration, robust underlying portfolio fundamentals, and substantial capital deployment runway, though near-term execution risks and structural cash drag temper immediate return optimization.

Change Materiality Assessment Transformative governance shift following WorxInvest's entry has driven significant board restructuring, cementing an independent majority alongside three new shareholder-nominated directors under Chairman Filip Dierckx's leadership. Massive strategic acceleration includes raising growth ambitions to double portfolio size from 1.7 billion EUR to 3.5 billion EUR over five years, structurally increasing return targets from 15 percent to 17.5 percent, and doubling average ticket sizes to 60 million EUR. Strategic platform restructuring involves completely discontinuing new Life Sciences investments while launching the evergreen Anchor structure, with flagship IT services holding Cegeka transferred as its foundational asset. Aggressive capital mobilization through a 243 million EUR rights issue provides substantial deployment runway to fund this accelerated growth strategy.
