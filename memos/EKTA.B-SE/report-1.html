Product Portfolio and Innovation Strategy
Elekta is undergoing a strategic pivot away from traditional hardware-centric development toward workflow-led, software-enabled solutions. This "Focused Innovation" approach prioritizes adaptive treatments and deeper
integration of the Elekta ONE ecosystem to improve clinical productivity and patient outcomes. By
transitioning to faster, customer-led innovation cycles, the company aims to enhance the predictability of its product roadmap and increase the return on its research and development investments.

Next-Generation Linear Accelerators: Evo and Unity
The core execution lever for Elekta's revitalized growth is the Elekta Evo, a highly versatile CT-Linac that recently
received FDA 510(k) clearance in January 2026. Evo integrates Iris, an AI-enhanced high-definition imaging technology, to deliver both online and offline adaptive radiation therapy. This platform allows clinicians to
visualize anatomical changes with exceptional clarity and adjust treatment plans in real-time, catering to the
growing market demand for hypofractionation. 21 • Jefferies Research 22 • Press Release 23 • ARS
From a commercial perspective, Evo is expected to support significant gross margin expansion, carrying a roughly 30% price premium with broadly unchanged cost of goods sold. 24 • Jefferies Research
Complementing the Evo in the premium personalization category is the Elekta Unity, the world's first high-field 1.5T MR-Linac. Unity combines diagnostic-grade magnetic resonance imaging with a linear accelerator to
provide superior soft-tissue visualization and real-time tumor tracking through its Comprehensive Motion
Management software. This capability enables clinicians to safely deliver high-dose radiation to complex targets, such as high-grade gliomas and prostate cancer, while sparing surrounding healthy tissue. The
system continues to gain traction among leading academic institutions, highlighted by a recent strategic collaboration with the Moffitt Cancer Center to anchor its MR-guided adaptive radiation therapy program.
