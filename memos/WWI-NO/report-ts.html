2.

9.
Steady infrastructure growth: The New Energy segment, primarily NorSea, grew revenue to USD 358 million in 2025 from USD 302 million in 2024
10.
Infrastructure customer concentration: Unlike Maritime Services, New Energy exhibits higher customer concentration with one customer representing 20% of segment income

Fleet and terminal scale: Wallenius Wilhelmsen operates 119 vessels and 8 terminals, serving leading global automotive and heavy equipment manufacturers
