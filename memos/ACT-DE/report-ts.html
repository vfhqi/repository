Section 3 – What the company does 11 findings

Section summary
  1. Alzchem operates a highly defensive, vertically integrated NCN chemistry monopoly that protects high-margin downstream specialty ingredients.

Vertically Integrated Value Chain

1.
Unique NCN chemistry platform: Alzchem is structured entirely around the Nitrogen-Carbon-Nitrogen (NCN) chemical chain. The company begins with lime, coal, and electricity to synthesize calcium carbide and calcium cyanamide in-house, then uses these backbones to manufacture complex downstream specialties like dicyandiamide, guanidine nitrate, and nitroguanidine
2.
Captive feedstock protection: By producing its own upstream precursors, the company completely insulates its specialty business from external raw material shocks
3.
Highly specialized segments: Operations are divided into three reportable segments: Specialty Chemicals (67% external revenue, 94% of group EBITDA), Basics & Intermediates, and Other & Holding

Product Moat and Customers

4.
Scarce specialty chemical blockbuster brands: Creapure (high-purity creatine monohydrate for sports nutrition), Creamino (patented animal feed additive), and nitroguanidine (defense propellant for 155mm artillery and airbags)
5.
Deeply defensive customer structure: Top ten customers account for approximately 33.00% of total group sales, governed by long-standing pricing agreements with pass-through raw material clauses
6.
High regulatory barriers: 356 granted patents and 970 registered trademarks defend proprietary synthesis methods. EFSA approvals and GMP certifications fortify competitive advantages
7.
Formidable entry moats: New entrants face years of qualification cycles and capital-intensive site permitting before supplying regulated food or defense contractors

Industry Landscape and Peer Positioning

8.
Consolidated global market structure: Key Far East competitors include Ningxia Calcium Cyanamide, Yinglite, and Beilite Chemical. In Western markets, Alzchem operates as the sole domestic manufacturer of dicyandiamide and nitroguanidine
9.
Strategic value over volume: In basic chemicals, the company consciously avoids price wars with Chinese commodity dumping by prioritizing pricing integrity
10.
Other side of the story: Intense Asian competitive capacity additions could erode Alzchem's premium pricing power if Chinese producers successfully pass strict Western regulatory hurdles
