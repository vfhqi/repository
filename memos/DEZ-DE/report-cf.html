EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (BLUF)

DEUTZ AG has undergone material structural transformation over the last twelve months, consolidating executive leadership under CEO Dr. Sebastian Schulte with his contract extended through December 2028, promoting Oliver Neu to CFO in October 2024, and expanding the Board of Management back to three members with the June 2026 appointment of Katharina Krüger as Chief Transformation Officer. The strategic reorganization eliminated the Chief Operating Officer role following Dr. Petra Mayer's December 2025 departure, signaling a deliberate streamlining of corporate structure to support the newly launched five-segment business unit model. However, this executive reshuffling coincides with frequent board changes since 2024 that could disrupt strategic execution, even as the company delivered robust revenue growth of 12.7% to €2.04 billion in fiscal 2025 alongside a 130 basis point margin expansion to 5.5%.
