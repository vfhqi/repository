  • Analysis: PWR-Transmission full-year margin of 12.3% (up from 11.0% in FY2024) shows progress but lags Prysmian's Transmission at 20.9% in Q4 -- the gap is ~860bps, wider than justified by scale alone [C+AS] #A
  • Analysis: Core Electrification segment posted a 13% margin in H2, missing consensus by ~50bps -- the underlying quality of the earnings beat is weaker than the headline suggests [AS] #A
