§6 — Margin + Profitability Outlook

  • J-front verdict: Margin expansion assumption is modest and credible in isolation, but almost entirely dependent on ONE Telia cost programme delivery — making it binary on execution risk [#J]

  • Consensus EBITDAAL margin: FY26E ~25–26%; FY27E ~26–27%; FY28E ~27–28% — ~100–200bps expansion over three years [#A]

  • Sub: European telecom sector median EBITDAAL margin ~35–40% (Deutsche Telekom, Orange) — TELIA-SE's ~26% is materially below sector median, reflecting higher network-sharing costs and less favourable market structure [#A] ❌

  • Consensus EBIT margin: FY26E ~10–11%; FY27E ~11–12% [#A]

  • Consensus net margin: FY26E ~7–8%; FY27E ~8–9% [#A]

  • Margin trend assumed: Gradual expansion; not aggressive — consensus is not assuming a step-change re-rating [#J]

  • Productivity assumption: ONE Telia cost programme targets SEK 2.0bn+ in cumulative savings by FY27; consensus broadly believes ~SEK 1.5–1.8bn is achievable (haircut applied) [#A]

  • Sub: [AS-Barclays] and [AS-ABN AMRO] model full programme delivery; [AS-Jefferies] and [AS-BNP Paribas] apply 20–25% haircut [#A]

  • Pricing assumption: No pricing power assumed in margin model by majority — margin improvement is pure cost/efficiency [#A]

  • Mix assumption: Modest positive mix shift from B2B services growth; [AS-UBS] and [AS-Citi] credit this as a margin tailwind [#A]

  • Operating leverage: Low — revenue growth of ~2% pa generates limited operating leverage; margin expansion is programme-driven, not leverage-driven [#J]

  • Fixed cost: FX headwinds on fixed-cost base (SEK depreciation against EUR-denominated capex) modestly offset savings; flagged by [AS-Nordea] and [AS-Morgan Stanley] [#A]

  • One-off vs structural: [AS-Jefferies] (Hold) raises the specific concern that a proportion of ONE Telia savings may be one-off efficiency timing (vendor renegotiation, headcount freeze) rather than permanent structural cost reduction — this is the key margin credibility risk [#J]

  • Margin downgrade L12M: FY26 EBITDAAL margin consensus trimmed ~80bps from mid-2024 levels; not dramatic but consistent with overall downward revision pattern [#A]

  • Sceptical read on margin outlook: Through what mechanism might ONE Telia savings be partly illusory — if ~30% of the SEK 2.0bn programme is timing/deferrals, FY27 margin exits at ~25–26% rather than 27%, and the EPS recovery story unravels; [AS-Jefferies] is the most credible voice on this risk [#J]
