⚠️ CRITICAL STRUCTURAL NOTE (read before proceeding)

Pre-revenue context: Nanobiotix is a clinical-stage radiobiology company. Its "revenue" through most of L3Y is dominated by collaboration income from the Bristol-Myers Squibb partnership (signed late 2019, $67M upfront + milestones + royalties). Commercial product revenue exists only in France/Europe (NBTXR3 / Hensify® in locally advanced soft tissue sarcoma, approved 2019) and is tiny relative to the BMS collaboration. Conventional earnings metrics (EBITDA, EBIT, EPS beats vs positive consensus) are structurally inapplicable. The analytical framework must be adapted: revenue = collaboration receipts + product revenue; "earnings quality" = cash burn management, milestone cadence, and R&D spend efficiency; "beat/miss" = collaboration revenue recognition timing vs analyst models.
