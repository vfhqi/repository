EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (BLUF)

Net Signal on Materiality of Change

The orderly succession of Johan Bladh to CEO on April 22, 2026, maintains operational continuity while allowing co-founder Fredrik Karlsson to concentrate as Deputy CEO on M&A deal execution, targeting the historical pace of 5-10 deals per year. Douglas Kressner was appointed as the new CFO at only 27 years old, having managed key IFRS reporting for the 2025 IPO. The company maintains its perpetual ownership model targeting European niche SMEs, committing to holding acquired businesses forever to attract family-run sellers. Röko completes five platform acquisitions annually on average at multiples below 8x EBITA, preserving capital allocation quality. The extreme decentralization limits direct operational oversight while 80% of total revenues remain exposed to cyclical industries.
