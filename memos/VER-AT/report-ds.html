Underwhelming initial guidance: Alongside the results on March 18, 2026, management guided to an FY2026 Net Income range of €0.90-1.20bn, which at its midpoint of €1.05bn missed sell-side consensus by 12%

Dividend shortfall announcement: Management proposed an FY2026 dividend payout of 45-55%, translating to a projected dividend per share range of €1.17 to €1.90, well below the published consensus of €1.79

Q1 EBITDA drop: For the first quarter of 2026, Verbund reported EBITDA of €535m, which was down 26% year-over-year, but slightly beat low expectations by 2%
5.
Q1 Net Income print: Reported Q1 Group Net Result reached €270m, which was down 32% year-over-year but beat the low consensus sample of €238m by 14%
6.
Guidance adjustment deviation: On May 13, 2026, Verbund adjusted its FY2026 Net Income guidance upward to €1.0-1.2bn, which was received positively but remained highly vulnerable due to deteriorating hydrology
