§8 — Governance Verdict + Sceptical Synthesis

J-front verdict: Cross-cutting bear synthesis: the operator + governance quality is genuine but the central bear case is not "bad governance" but rather "good governance that is optimised for a specific model" — and the model itself is the latent risk
