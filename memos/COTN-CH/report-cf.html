EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (BLUF) 51 findings

Comet Holding AG has undertaken material strategic and governance transformation , transitioning board oversight to semiconductor-focused expertise under Chairman Benjamin Loh while stabilizing executive leadership with CFO Christian Witt's arrival in early 2025 and demonstrating improved organizational culture through voluntary employee turnover declining to 5.4% in 2025. The company has completed a fundamental structural pivot toward semiconductor-exposed verticals through its updated Focus Strategy 2030, executing an all-in semiconductor approach while launching a systematic 2026 restructuring program targeting structurally improved operating margins and capacity scalability, all achieved without any active activist presence in the shareholder base.

The external environment presents meaningful cyclical tailwinds , with global semiconductor capital expenditures inflecting to drive accelerated demand for wafer fabrication equipment through 2027, enabling Comet to leverage its Synertia platform to capture market share from competitors in the RF power market while mitigating supply chain risks through Penang manufacturing capacity expansion and benefiting from strong cyclical book-to-bill inflection driven by inventory restocking at major OEMs. However, severe headwinds persist from pending regulatory shifts like the MATCH Act presenting technological export and compliance risks in China, compounded

by severe currency headwinds from USD devaluations that continue to heavily compress Swiss export margins.
