EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (BLUF)

Arcadis NV is executing a comprehensive operational overhaul under newly appointed CEO Heather Polinsky and CFO Simon Crowe , marking a decisive strategic inflection point toward higher-margin energy transition, water optimization, and nuclear advisory services following years of structural underperformance in traditional environmental restoration and cyclical real estate exposure . The company's "Accelerating a Planet Positive Future" strategy fundamentally reorients the business away from legacy environmental restoration toward sustainable solutions including energy transition, water optimization, and nuclear advisory capabilities , supported by an ambitious commitment to train 2,500 energy experts by 2027 through a dedicated Energy Transition Academy . Despite this strategic reset, Arcadis continues to trade at a significant 25% to 29% valuation discount versus pure-play engineering peers on FY26E multiples, presenting a compelling re-rating opportunity as execution gains traction and the historically wide 40% to 50% discount narrows .
