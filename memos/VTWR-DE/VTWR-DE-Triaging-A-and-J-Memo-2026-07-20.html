1. External web search (2026-07-20). Multiple independent sources (Deutsche Börse Xetra press release, MarketScreener, Vodafone corporate newsroom, Wikipedia, Capacity/DCD trade press) confirm: Vodafone entered a co-control partnership with GIP and KKR in November 2022; the partnership completed 23 March 2023; the accompanying voluntary public takeover offer completed with Oak Holdings GmbH owning 89.3% of shares; Vantage Towers AG shares were delisted from the Frankfurt Stock Exchange effective upon the expiry of 9 May 2023. As of the most recent data found (March 2025), Oak Holdings holds ~89.32% and the company remains private. No source found any evidence of relisting through mid-2026.

Note for the record: this same digest continues to synthesise operational/financial detail through FY25/FY26 and CEO successions as late as May 2026 — Vantage Towers continues to exist and report as a private operating company (still majority Vodafone-linked, still consolidated/associate-accounted at Vodafone group level), which is why research content keeps being generated on it.
