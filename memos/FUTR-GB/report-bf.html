Defensive strategic pivots and execution credibility

Li Ying launched an eleven-point innovation roadmap in September 2025 to transform B2C brands into channelagnostic destinations, with the core strategic initiative focusing on pulling direct traffic via social commerce, newsletters, and direct-to-consumer apps, the launch of "Future Optic" leveraging the company's high organic visibility inside Large Language Models to sell tailored AI-visibility packages, and the "Future Collab" initiative partnering with social media creators, offering them tech stack access in exchange for revenue sharing. The £39.9 million acquisition of SheerLuxe adds a platform-agnostic asset with no Google traffic reliance, drawing 6

million users across TikTok, Instagram, and podcasts, accelerating direct social monetization. Peer precedents demonstrate that digital publishers can successfully transition to intent-driven direct formats, with scale competitors like Dotdash Meredith proving that proprietary first-party targeting can drive double-digit ad growth, and Future's targeted brand pruning and £20 million efficiency program providing clear avenues to defend margins. However, the fundamental concern remains that heroic direct audience initiatives may fail to scale in time to offset the rapid, algorithmic decay of high-margin B2C programmatic traffic, permanently impairing the group's historic platform profitability.
