EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (BLUF) 30 findings

Belimo Holding AG operates as a specialized, highly proﬁtable manufacturer of energy-eﬃcient ﬁeld devices for commercial HVAC systems, maintaining dominant global market positions in damper actuators and control valves while capturing exceptional returns on capital through an asset-light business model . The company's three product lines–damper actuators (42% of 2025 sales), control valves (53%), and sensors (5%)–collectively generated CHF 1,120.81 million in net sales during ﬁscal year 2025, with control valves emerging as the fastest- growing segment . Financial performance has been exceptional, with local currency sales growth accelerating to 23.30% in 2025 and EBIT margins expanding to 20.80%, surpassing the company's upgraded medium-term guidance corridor . Belimo's capital eﬃciency stands out dramatically, achieving a 36.30% return on capital employed and 27.80% return on invested capital in 2025, substantially outclassing diversiﬁed HVAC conglomerate peers . The company serves two distinct channels–contracting at approximately 55% of sales and original equipment manufacturers at 45%–with an exceptionally fragmented customer base where the largest single client represents only 5% of group revenues . Belimo's asset-light manufacturing strategy outsources approximately 88% of production costs while limiting internal operations to 8% ﬁnal assembly and 4% customization, enabling elite capital returns . The broader building automation market, estimated at USD 120 billion globally with baseline growth of approximately 4%, is experiencing accelerated demand from AI-driven data center construction, with US hyperscalers revising cloud capex estimates to 82% year-on-year growth reaching USD 815 billion in 2026 .
