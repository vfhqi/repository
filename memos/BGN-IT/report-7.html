Banca Generali is navigating a complex transition from a net interest income driven growth model to one anchored by structural fee-based recurring income and long-term distribution partnerships. While the FY2025 results showed a consolidation phase with headline profit growth of 3%, the underlying 7% growth in recurring net profit suggests a resilient core. [1] The market is currently focused on whether the transformational Alleanza partnership can offset the structural headwinds of a stable interest rate environment and intensifying competition in the Italian wealth management space. [2] Below is a detailed assessment of the key share price drivers for BGN over the next 24 months.1 • ARS 2 • Morgan Stanley
