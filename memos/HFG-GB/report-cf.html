EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (BLUF)

net signal on strategic change is constructive but execution-dependent. A completed strategic review refocuses group capital on core retail meat and fresh prepared foods while seeking margin-accretive growth , and strategic divestments of non-core foodservice and software assets have unlocked capital to fund major multi-year international expansions . The One Hilton operating model creates clearer regional accountability through dedicated East and West COOs, isolating volatile seafood and vegan businesses outside the core structure. Portfolio simplification through the Fairfax Meadow disposal and Foods Connected sell-down crystallised approximately £80 million in cash proceeds while maintaining operational software contracts.

Nevertheless, the ambitious parallel deployment of capital across Canada, Poland, and Saudi Arabia introduces meaningful execution risk and near-term margin pressure.
