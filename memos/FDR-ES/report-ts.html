1.

2.
Water scarcity transition risk: climate change-driven water shortages and subsequent pool water restrictions present a material transition risk, with management estimating a potential sales hit of up to €200.00m
3.
Channel disintermediation: a rise in direct-to-consumer online sales of cordless robotic cleaners could bypass professional B2B channels, eroding Fluidra's high-margin premium cleaner segment
4.
Promotional pricing pressure: intense competition from peers or low-cost Chinese entrants could force Fluidra to increase rebates or dealer incentives, compressing its adjusted EBITDA margin
5.
Weather dependency: unseasonably cold or wet summers in the Northern Hemisphere represent a structural "false friend," as a short-term volume drop can mask underlying market share gains

Early Warning Signals and Mitigants

6.
Single failure mode: the most critical failure mode is a prolonged credit freeze that halts home equity line of credit activity, which has grown at a 9.00% CAGR since 2022 to finance remodel projects
