EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (BLUF) 24 findings

Business foundation and core value proposition

1.
Core technology: Chemometec develops, manufactures, and markets high-precision fluorescence-based cell counting instruments and associated consumables , employing a highly profitable, scalable razor-and-blade commercial structure where consumables and services represented over 75% of total sales in recent periods
2.
Product portfolio: The instrument portfolio includes legacy cell counters (NC-200, NC-202, NC-250, NC-3000) and the new automated XcytoMatic platform (XM30, XM40, XM50) with integrated artificial intelligence, where XcytoMatic models can analyze up to 24 samples simultaneously versus two for legacy models
3.
Switching costs and stickiness: Cell counters are integrated directly into pharmaceutical research and commercial therapy manufacturing with validated clinical workflows requiring FDA-approved protocols, making replacement of validated instruments extremely difficult, effectively locking in customers once automated systems are validated

Financial profile versus life science peers

4.
Elite margin profile: Chemometec grew revenue at an exceptional 36.00% CAGR from 2016/17 to 2021/22, with gross margins consistently remaining best-in-class at over 90.00% for the past decade and EBITDA margins consistently exceeding 50.00% from 2021/22 through 2024/25
5.
World-class returns: Return on invested capital has consistently exceeded 60.00% over the last five years, peaking at an extraordinary 107.20% pre-tax in 2021/22, while adjusted pre-tax ROCE reached 61.20% in 2021/22 before settling at 34.10% in 2024/25
6.
versus peers Sartorius (29.70% EBITDA, ~9% ROIC), Lonza (31.60% EBITDA, 11.50% ROIC), and Repligen (21.00% EBITDA, 8.50% ROIC) due to its highly automated, consolidated manufacturing of disposable cassettes combined with an asset-light razor-and-blade business model
