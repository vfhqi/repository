  • Sceptical bullet: The bearish regulatory read is that EU permitting acceleration targets are member-state commitments with weak enforcement mechanisms — France, in particular, has a long history of regulatory intent outpacing implementation; if French grid connection queues do not clear by 2025-2026 as assumed in Voltalia's capacity target, the 3.3 GW goal faces a structural rather than operational constraint, and Brazil FX + political risk is systematically underweighted in consensus models that assume a stable BRL contribution [#J]
