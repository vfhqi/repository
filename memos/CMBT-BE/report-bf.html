EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (BLUF) 34 findings

Section summary
  1. CMB.TECH operates the largest listed diversified shipping platform globally, building long-term strategic advantage through first-mover investments in dual-fuel ammonia and hydrogen supply chain infrastructure. The business balances highly cyclical spot-heavy revenue exposure with a USD 3.26 billion contracted backlog, while pursuing an ambitious technology-led diversification strategy to transition into a low-carbon maritime leader. Dual-fuel vessel technology establishes technical switching barriers that protect charterers against escalating carbon compliance penalties and safeguard fleet residual asset values, but capital efficiency ratios lag pure-play tanker peers and cash flow conversion remains severely pressured by heavy shipyard installment commitments. The widening of the group's competitive advantage requires continuous active capital deployments and depends critically on global emission legislation acceleration rather than self-reinforcing mechanical scale advantages.
