The leadership transition to CEO Géraldine Picaud in March 2024 and CFO Marta Vlatchkova in May 2024 has catalyzed material operational transformation across SGS. Management decreased overhead headcount while eliminating legacy matrix layers that duplicated roles , accelerating accountability and performance-driven culture. The magnitude of structural change is high , with execution risks concentrated in ATS integration and sustained margin expansion under persistent currency headwinds.
