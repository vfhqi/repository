#5 – Finnish Market Turnaround: MEDIUM plausibility. Structural recovery of the Finnish store network after many years of severe underperformance, with Finland delivering a surprisingly robust 9% organic growth rate in the fourth quarter, though the market has historically struggled to sustain long-term profitability and scale
