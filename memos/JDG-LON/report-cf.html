EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (BLUF)

Judges Scientific completed a multi-year CEO succession in February 2026, transitioning from founder David Cicurel to Dr Tim Prestidge, while Cicurel retained active M&A advisory duties as Non-Executive Chair. The new executive team brings significant peer experience, with Prestidge spending eight years as divisional CEO at Halma and Dr Ian Wilcock joining from Oxford Instruments as Group Commercial Director. Management established an Executive Committee to broaden operational oversight beyond the historic founder-dependent model, preventing board-level bottlenecks across the growing portfolio of twenty-five autonomous subsidiaries. While average employee tenure stands at seven years, voluntary staff turnover rose to 19% in 2025, exceeding the UK average of 15%.
