EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (BLUF)

Core Business Model and Strategic Positioning

Carmila operates a highly defensible grocery-anchored shopping centre model co-located adjacent to Carrefour hypermarkets , providing predictable physical retail traffic across 250 shopping centres in France (70% of assets), Spain (21%), and Italy. This co-location strategy ensures one in three people in France and Spain lives within 20 minutes of a Carmila centre, creating exceptionally high traffic resilience focused on everyday essentials like discount retail and healthcare rather than cyclical discretionary fashion. Carmila earned the title of retailers' landlord of choice in 2025, with 85% of centres occupying leader or co-leader positions in their local markets and maintaining high financial occupancy at 96.5% through strong leasing execution. The €300M Galimmo acquisition added 51 shopping centres at an accretive 10% acquisition yield , generating an internal rate of return exceeding 40% and demonstrating the scalability of Carmila's operational platform.
