EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (BLUF)

Change Materiality and Strategic Inflection

Orderly CEO transition to Stefan Goethaert on 1 July 2023 represents a shift toward professionalized non-family operational management while the Colruyt family retains board-level control, with no active activist presence given the 69.24% voting control by Korys and the founding family. French retail divestment marks the most material strategic change, as Colruyt signed binding agreements to sell 81 integrated stores and 44 fuel stations to Groupement Mousquetaires, which is projected to add 40-50 bps to EBIT margins by eliminating over €20.00 million in annual losses. Accelerated shareholder returns via a new €50 million share buyback programme and the cancellation of 4,000,000 treasury shares signal management's confidence in post-restructuring cash generation. Operational strategy pivots to aggressive process simplification, with 600 FTEs in the Indian offshore IT division to mitigate Belgian labor costs and AI-driven easy checkout systems rolled out to 100 stores by February 2026.
