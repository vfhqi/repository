RESULT FOR QUERY

CapEx model to a hybrid approach emphasizing "customer intimacy" and subscription-based revenue (AaaS).

robust recovery with a 12% revenue beat against consensus and a record order intake. However, extreme partner
concentration remains a primary risk, with four distribution partners now controlling 74% of total revenue, up from
65% in 2024.
