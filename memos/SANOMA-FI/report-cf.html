EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (BLUF) 1 findings

Sanoma is executing a strategic pivot toward becoming a pure-play European K-12 learning provider through targeted acquisitions in Spain, Poland, and the Netherlands, while optimizing its capital structure by refinancing a EUR 150 million hybrid bond with senior debt and pursuing a digital-first media model in Finland . The company delivered adjusted operating profit of EUR 188 million in 2025 with group margins expanding to 14.4% , up from 13.4% in 2024 , driven primarily by the completion of Program Solar which delivered EUR 55 million in annual cost savings starting in 2026 . Leadership continuity is assured through CEO Rob Kolkman's orderly internal succession , while board refreshment and an improved employee Net Promoter Score of +5 in 2025 from -5 in 2024 signal cultural stabilization following significant restructuring efforts.
