EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (BLUF)

ALSO Holding AG operates a hybrid three-segment business model that is successfully transitioning from lowmargin hardware transactions toward high-margin recurring digital cloud platform services, with leadership continuity maintained through Wolfgang Krainz's CEO appointment in May 2024 and robust financial delivery achieving €15.2 billion in net sales and €286 million EBITDA during 2025 . The company demonstrates competitive positioning against global distributors including TD Synnex and software specialists like SoftwareOne, achieving superior working capital efficiency with negative three cashdays and delivering ROCE of 16.10% in 2025, though adjusted ROCE reached 25.20% excluding the dilutive impact of the Westcoast acquisition . ALSO occupies a pivotal aggregator position within the IT value chain, balancing capital-intensive distribution with asset-light digital platforms that capture superior transactional economics, though the profit pool remains highly polarized with cloud infrastructure and managed service tiers accumulating the greatest value while traditional middle-tier reselling faces structural margin compression . The customer base spans approximately 120,000 resellers with substantial technical switching costs created through custom API integrations and automated billing engines, though platform lock-in remains vulnerable to vendor consolidation as Microsoft continues restricting its indirect provider ecosystem .
