  • Sceptical bullet: The bearish read on earnings quality is that the strong EBITDA-to-OCF conversion masks the real constraint: free cash flow after debt service and capex is thin or negative during the capacity build phase — ERG is consuming cash to grow, and the dividend may strain balance sheet capacity if EBITDA recovery is delayed [#J]

  • Strongest bear case: ERG's investment thesis rests on a convergence of three independent drivers (power price recovery, on-schedule capacity additions, manageable refinancing at acceptable rates) — the probability of all three materialising simultaneously is materially lower than the bull thesis implies [#J]
