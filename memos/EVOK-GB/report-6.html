Strategy Architecture and Objectives
Evoke's strategic framework is built upon a fundamental bifurcation of its geographic footprint into "Core" and
"Optimise" markets. The five Core Markets—the UK, Italy, Spain, Romania, and Denmark—represent
approximately 90% of total group revenue. Within these specific regions, the company targets podium positions
and sustainable profitable growth by leveraging local expertise, high barriers to entry, and established regulatory
frameworks. Conversely, the Optimise markets are managed strictly to maximize cash flow and value
generation. This disciplined resource allocation strategy includes exiting unprofitable regions, such as the US
B2C market, to prioritize risk-adjusted returns in core jurisdictions. 1 5 • ARS
