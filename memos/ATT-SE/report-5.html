  • Gross margin — current vs history: Staff cost ratio (primary COGS equivalent in care services) improved ~100–150bp year-on-year in Q1 2025; best improvement in 3 years [#A]
  • Q1 2025 staff cost % of revenue: estimated ~72–74% vs ~74–76% in Q1 2024

  • Pre-pandemic (FY2019) staff cost ratio was ~70–72%; gap to normalisation is closing but not closed

  • EBIT margin — current vs history: Group EBIT margin estimated ~8.0–8.5% in Q1 2025 vs ~6.5–7.0% in Q1 2024; +150bp year-on-year improvement [#A]

  • Margin direction: Expanding — 3-year trajectory shows trough in FY2022, gradual improvement in FY2023–FY2024, acceleration in Q1 2025 [#A]

  • The inflection is progressing faster than sell-side consensus models expected 12 months ago

  • One-off costs or benefits: No material one-offs flagged in Q1 2025 release; adjusted and reported margins converge — clean earnings quality [#A]

  • In contrast, Q3–Q4 2024 had modest restructuring charges associated with Finland efficiency programme; these are now largely expensed

  • Peer comparison: No D-BATCH-25 peer data injected; comparisons based on public disclosures only; flag thin_peer_context on all peer references in this section [#A]
  • Ambea (ATT-SE's closest Swedish private elderly care peer): Q4 2024/Q1 2025 results showed similar occupancy recovery trajectory; EBIT margin ~9–10% (above Attendo's current level, suggesting Attendo has further to travel)
