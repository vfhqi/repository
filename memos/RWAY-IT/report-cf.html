EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (BLUF)

Rai Way maintains highly stable senior leadership under CEO Roberto Cecatto, appointed April 2023, alongside CFO Adalberto Pellegrino and an experienced executive team including Giancarlo Benucci and Andrea Moretti, though the Board of Directors term expires with FY2025 financial statement approval, introducing potential governance transition risks in 2026. This orderly succession from former CEO Aldo Mancino was designed to strengthen strategic coordination with majority stakeholder RAI, which exercises dominant management and coordination influence through Chairman Giuseppe Pasciucco. Internal cultural metrics demonstrate sustained excellence, including nine consecutive years of Top Employers certification, 28.60% year-on-year increase in training hours during 2025, and comprehensive employee satisfaction monitoring covering 100% of the workforce.

The most transformative strategic option remains industrial consolidation with domestic peer EI Towers. Evaluating potential aggregation with EI Towers remains the primary industrial consolidation option, with the memorandum of understanding between reference shareholders extended to June 15, 2026. The combined entity leverage would rise to approximately 2.0x Net Debt/EBITDA, well below peer medians, while Morgan Stanley estimated potential synergy value of €320 million to €370 million net present value. However, execution plausibility remains low, dependent on complex state broadcaster and antitrust clearances.

Forward EPS drivers center on six core mechanisms with varying plausibility and timing profiles. Traditional core broadcasting expansion and inflation-linked pricing deliver the highest plausibility and confidence, providing an estimated +1.50% and +1.20% YoY impact respectively. Edge data center rollout carries medium plausibility with an estimated +0.50% impact, while the Rome hyperscale facility represents a potentially transformative +2.00% impact but faces medium plausibility due to municipal permit bottlenecks. Solar photovoltaic generation offers high plausibility with an estimated +0.80% impact, while merger with EI Towers represents the largest potential catalyst at +15.00% but carries low plausibility dependent on government decree. The compound probability of all six drivers delivering simultaneously is approximately 25%.
