2. Company Description & Context

Elekta AB (STO: EKTA-B.ST, FactSet: EKTA.B-SE) is a Swedish medical device company specializing in radiation therapy and radiosurgery solutions for cancer and brain disorder treatment. Headquarters in Stockholm, ~4,700 employees globally, operating in 120+ countries. The company is a pure-play oncology focused on three core pillars: (1) equipment (linear accelerators, including the flagship Elekta Unity MR-Linac and newer Elekta Evo CT-Linac), (2) software (MOSAIQ oncology information system, Monaco treatment planning, Elekta ONE platform), and (3) services (support, training, consumables). FY2025 revenue composition: Solutions (hardware + software licenses) ~58% and Services ~42%, with software and recurring services now contributing ~45% of total revenue—a deliberate shift toward stickier, higher-margin recurring streams.

Financial scale and profile: FY2025 revenue of SEK 18.0B (~USD 1.62B equivalent at ~11.1 SEK/USD), representing stagnation versus FY2024. Adjusted EBIT margin of 11.6% on a SEK 279M cost reduction run-rate achieved in FY2025. Net debt position historically modest; the company has been investing in R&D and M&A (though the FY2025 impairment signals disciplined R&D reassessment). Capital structure: listed on Nasdaq Stockholm, freely traded OTC in the U.S. (EKTAY, EKTAF).
