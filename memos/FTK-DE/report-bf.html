EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (BLUF)

FlatexDEGIRO operates a highly scalable, transaction-led retail brokerage model with significant operating leverage, though the transition to a unified IT platform under Oliver Behrens aims to unlock efficiency while intense marketing pressure in Germany tests profitability. The company delivers strong organic growth and high operating leverage but exhibits lower profitability margins than Nordic peers due to a transaction-heavy mix, with Avanza and AJ Bell leading on operating margins due to scale-driven asset custody fees.
