EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (BLUF)

Nolato AB presents an orderly strategic transition anchored by incoming CEO Anders Björklund, who brings deep industrial experience from his nearly four-year leadership of the Engineered Solutions division and prior roles at Kanthal, Gunnebo, Volvo, and Ericsson. The company exhibits structural shifts accelerating toward highermargin medical cleanroom capacity while deliberately downsizing volatile consumer electronics and vaporiser heating product exposure. Group EBITA margins have recovered from a historic trough of 7.3% in 2023 to 11.3% in 2025 and 11.0% in Q1 2026 , demonstrating credible progress toward the upgraded mid-term target exceeding 12% established in March 2025.

The net strategic signal is decisively positive , characterized by a SEK 665 million Medical Solutions investment program in 2025, primarily funding a SEK 600 million Hungarian pen injector facility that positions Nolato to capture exponential growth in obesity and diabetes drug delivery devices . This SEK 700 million annual sales contract for diabetes and obesity pen injectors capitalizes on growing global demand for weight loss and diabetes treatments driving exponential growth in autoinjector volumes. Management confirmed validation deliveries continued in Q1 2026 with commercial volumes scheduled to begin in late Q2 2026, with DNB Carnegie estimating the contract will boost Medical Solutions' organic sales by 2-4 percentage points annually. Complementing this medical expansion, the Engineered Solutions Materials division registered a 15% sales surge in Q1 2026 driven by data center shielding demand, with Materials now accounting for 21% of Engineered Solutions sales, up from 18%.
