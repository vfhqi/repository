§9 — Sceptical Synthesis (cross-cutting)

The L3Y track record is genuine, consistent, and operationally respectable. The bearish case is not that management is fraudulent or incompetent — it is that the headline delivery conceals an organic deceleration that has been masked by acquisitions and commodity tailwinds, both of which have natural limits.

  • Sceptical verdict: The strongest bearish case is organic deceleration dressed as structural growth — the ~11% reported revenue CAGR masks ~5-6% organic; if M&A pacing slows or UK recovery underdelivers, headline metrics will expose the gap [#J]

  • Reverse-engineered bear: Adjusted EPS growth of ~11% L3Y is partially acquisition-funded; strip out M&A contribution, and organic EPS growth is probably ~6-7% — at ~20x P/E, that supports a 15-18% IRR at best, not 25%+ [#J]
