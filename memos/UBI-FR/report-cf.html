EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (BLUF)

base. 30 (India) structural setup resembles a high-risk turnaround dependent on flawless multi-year execution against deteriorating industry economics. The joint probability of delivering the bull case is significantly diluted by chronic game delays, with the setup risking a chronic value trap if new titles continue to miss quality benchmarks. Success requires simultaneous achievement of aggressive cost reduction, pristine AAA launch execution, sustained live services engagement, successful refinancing at reasonable terms, and retention of critical creative talent–a compound probability that historical delivery patterns suggest is implausibly low. The forward path is further constrained by proposed US tariffs on non-US cultural industries threatening European game publishers, as North America represents 45% of revenues , and disruptive world models that could compress environment creation cycles and erode the competitive moat of large-scale open worlds. Without rapid stabilization of both operational execution and capital structure, Ubisoft faces escalating probability of strategic surrender through distressed asset sales or involuntary recapitalization.
