B.1 Business Description 7 findings
Business Description -- A plain-English description of what the company does, how it makes money, and its primary market.
1.
Diversified electrical connectivity specialist. Volex designs, manufactures, and delivers cable assemblies and power systems across five segments: Complex Industrial Technology (AI data centres and defence), Electric Vehicles, Off-Highway (Murat Ticaret / military vehicles), Medical, Consumer Electricals.
2.
Scale. Revenue USD 1,087m FY2025 (from USD 391m FY2020 — 22.8% CAGR); organic constant-currency growth 13.0% H1 FY2026; 23 manufacturing plants in 25 nations — Sivas (Turkey), Batam (Indonesia), Tijuana (Mexico) as primary low-cost hubs.
3.
Margin and returns. Underlying EBIT 9.8% H1 FY2026 (top of 9–10% range, maintained for six consecutive years); ROCE 20.2% LTM Sept 2025 — 500bps+ above UK mid-cap industrials (<15%); 97% pre-capex cash conversion (5yr average); underlying gross margin 23.3% H1 FY2026.
4.
Structural mix shift. Moving from commodity power cords to complex harnesses: content value 5–10x per unit uplift; 70%+ of products now complex; EBITA margins expanded ~900bps over the past decade via this migration; medium-term target 12% EBIT.
5.
Capital allocation. 12 acquisitions since FY2019 at blended 5.2x EBITDA (including Murat Ticaret at 5.3x, operating at ~20% EBITDA at acquisition); withdrew from TT Electronics bid when valuation exceeded internal hurdles; £40m buyback launched April 2026; covenant leverage 1.0x (vs 3.0x covenant ceiling).
6.
Management. Nat Rothschild transitioned from Executive Chairman to CEO late 2025 (role separation); scaled Volex from <$400m (2015) to >$1bn; Dave Webster (former ECI CEO) as new Non-Exec Chairman; decentralised regional COO model; sandbagger archetype — systematic guidance beats across FY2022–FY2026.
7.
Investment style. Event-driven compounder: structural AI/EV demand upcycle × self-help margin expansion × Main Market re-rating catalyst (AIM→LSE, planned before August 4, 2026 → FTSE 250 September review).
