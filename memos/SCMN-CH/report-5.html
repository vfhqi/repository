  • Fibre regulatory timeline (CQ5): Switzerland fibre rollout at ~55% — ahead of most EU peers (EU average ~50-55%); Swiss regulatory timeline broadly on track; Swisscom's early 5G and fibre lead is a structural advantage vs peers [#J]
  • Peer yield comparison: Swisscom ~5% dividend yield; Proximus ~7-8% (but stressed balance sheet); KPN ~4-5%; Deutsche Telekom ~3%; Swisscom yield attractive relative to risk profile [#A]
  • Leverage vs peers (CQ4): 2.4x ND/EBITDA puts Swisscom temporarily at par with Deutsche Telekom; below Proximus (~2.8-3.0x); above KPN (~2.3x); pre-acquisition Swisscom at ~1.5x was best-in-class — leverage advantage partially surrendered [#A]
  • State ownership factor: 51% Swiss Confederation ownership provides explicit dividend underpinning; limits strategic flexibility (no transformative M&A, limited buyback capacity) but also limits downside catastrophe risk [#J]
