  • Achievable from L3Y: The only forward driver with L3Y evidential support is RAB growth; grid capex is visibly accelerating, regulatory mechanism is established, and management commentary is consistent — but RAB growth alone only delivers low-single-digit EPS accretion [#A]

  • Sceptical read: What's the bearish read on forward plausibility? §9 — Sceptical Synthesis

  • Reverse-engineered bear: The strongest bearish case is that FY2024 (~€5.80 EPS) represents peak normalised earnings, not a floor — if German industrial electricity demand continues to decline, distribution volumes compress, and tariff review is unfavourable, EPS could decline further toward €5.00-5.20, compressing the investment case below any reasonable IRR threshold [#J]
