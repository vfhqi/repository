EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (BLUF)

Convatec has successfully pivoted to a highly predictable, repeatable chronic care model , transforming from a stagnant private equity-backed business into a compounding medical technology platform anchored by four defensive divisions –Advanced Wound Care, Ostomy Care, Continence Care, and Infusion Care–that collectively generate over 900 million consumable products annually .

The company operates within highly consolidated oligopolies where Coloplast, Hollister, and Convatec collectively control the vast majority of the global ostomy care market , benefiting from extreme barriers to entry driven by stringent regulatory approvals, sticky clinical relationships with specialized nurses, and multi-year Group Purchasing Organisation contracts.

Leadership has stabilized under CEO Jonny Mason, who worked at Convatec for over four years as former CFO during the turnaround, preserving commercial execution and margin expansion momentum following former CEO Karim Bitar's tragic passing.

The Accelerate strategy targets 6% to 8% organic revenue growth annually starting in fiscal year 2027 , underpinned by nearly doubled R&D spending from 2.7% of sales in 2018 to approximately 5.0% currently, delivering eight major product launches between 2022 and 2025 .

The business model exhibits exceptional revenue visibility , with over 90.00% of revenues generated by non-discretionary, recurring chronic care medical consumables , insulated from macroeconomic cyclicality by aging demographics and irreversible clinical needs.
