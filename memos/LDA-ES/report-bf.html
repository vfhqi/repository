Section summary
  1. the direct-to-consumer digital model avoids high branch costs and broker commissions, driving superior operational efficiency compared to traditional intermediaries.

Linea Directa Aseguradora operates a pure direct-to-consumer insurance model without any physical branch network or traditional broker intermediaries, centralizing all customer contact points at its Tres Cantos headquarters and thereby avoiding high distribution costs that burden branch-heavy Spanish peers . This structural cost advantage enables the company to pass internal savings directly to customers while maintaining superior operational efficiency. The firm's strategic focus remains anchored on motor insurance as the dominant revenue pillar , supplemented by targeted diversification into home and health segments, all distributed through highly automated digital channels that handle over ninety percent of policyholder interactions.
