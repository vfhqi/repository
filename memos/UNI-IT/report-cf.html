EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (BLUF)

Unipol executed a transformational corporate consolidation by completing the merger by incorporation of UnipolSai effective December 31, 2024, and subsequently approved the "Stronger | Faster | Better" 2025-2027 Strategic Plan on March 27, 2025. The merger streamlines governance, simplifies decision-making, reduces funding costs, and strengthens group solvency, representing the single most material structural change in recent corporate history. Leadership continuity is preserved through the reappointment of CEO Matteo Laterza on April 29, 2025, with his mandate extending through the approval of the 2027 financial statements, ensuring strategic execution consistency across the plan horizon. Digital transformation accelerated with the launch of the Unica modular retail offering platform in June 2025, securing approximately 237,000 policies signed by year-end, while a Salesforce CRM pilot across 13 agencies in 2025 is scheduled for full network rollout in the first half of 2026. The confluence of structural simplification, leadership stability, digital platform deployment, and ambitious financial targets underscores high materiality of strategic repositioning, positioning Unipol for sustained margin expansion and bancassurance-led premium growth.
