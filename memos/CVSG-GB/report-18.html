- Assessment: Weak-to-moderate — no evidence of relentless drive or boundary-pushing energy in public record; the M&A pause is a disciplined decision but also a passive decision (stopping doing something); no evidence of aggressive market-share pursuit, bold hiring of outlier talent, or visible personal energy driving the organisation
