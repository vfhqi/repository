Technology Disruption and Data Center Risks. AI data centers rapidly accelerate their transition toward 800 VDC power architectures, drastically reducing the physical volume of traditional power cables required per server rack. This technological shift permanently limits Prysmian's ability to sustain its projected growth trajectory in high-margin energy cables. However, the technological roadmap for higher voltage architectures faces near-term supply chain bottlenecks, and hyperscalers possess both the motive to reduce power loss and the market power to dictate architectural standards only gradually. Named hyperscalers have not yet publicly announced standardized 800 VDC reference designs for new AI clusters, limiting the immediate threat window.
