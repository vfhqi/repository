Net FX Exposure Assessment and Resilience Verdict

The Group's operational cost base is almost completely Euro-denominated, reflecting its heavy geographical concentration in the European Union, with trade payables in Euros standing at €141.60m or 99.53% of total outstanding payables in 2024, while personnel expenses and facility costs are fully paid in Euros across its core Netherlands and Finland production bases. The high overlap between EUR-denominated sales and EUR-denominated costs forms an exceptionally strong organic natural hedge, although transaction risk primarily arises when USD-denominated battery packs are imported for EUR-denominated system sales, while management maintains Euro balances as its functional currency, reducing cash translation risks on year-end balances.

Alfen maintains a deliberate unhedged posture, as the company does not use foreign exchange contracts or financial derivatives to manage transaction or translation exposures, with the board determining through formal risk assessments that currency risks do not warrant active hedging, leaving the corporate treasury derivative-free which avoids financial instrument transaction fees but increases unhedged tail volatility, as the group has no derivative assets or liabilities recorded on its consolidated balance sheet. This unhedged stance contrasts sharply with peers, as Siemens Energy enforces a strict policy requiring units to hedge 75% to 100% of net exposures and applies hedge accounting to forward contracts protecting currency fluctuations, while Itron utilizes monthly foreign exchange forward contracts and maintained 37 forward contracts in Q1 2026 with notionals ranging from $100,000 to $28.7 million, ABB requires subsidiaries to hedge contracted exposures through intercompany derivative contracts with Corporate Treasury, Schneider Electric manages foreign exchange risk centrally through its Fair Value Natural Hedge program, and Landis+Gyr uses foreign currency forward contracts with gross outstanding derivative notional rising to USD 491.3 million in March 2026 from USD 253.8 million.
