EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (BLUF)

CFO Marie-Anne Popp brings institutional financial discipline and capital structure optimization, having deployed €1.84 billion in Talabat IPO proceeds to execute convertible bond buybacks, maintaining a robust €2.11 billion cash balance and reducing leverage toward the targeted peer-relative comfort zone of under 2.5x EBITDA . The supervisory board exhibits balanced co-determination with eight members under equal shareholder-employee parity, with all eight members classified as independent under the German Corporate Governance Code . However, activist presence includes Sachem Head founder Scott Ferguson serving on the board, while Uber holds a 36.83% economic stake, and Aspex Management previously built a 14.55% stake and demanded the CEO's removal, leading Östberg to agree to resign by March 2027 . 11

Expert consensus reveals severe operational misalignment between Berlin and regional brands, with former executives characterizing Östberg as managing the group like a transaction-focused private equity fund, where Berlin centralized control frequently replaces local founders with headquarters-appointed managers within two years of acquisition, leaving local leadership teams feeling highly unmotivated by rigid strategic controls . In South Korea, while Woowa Brothers' Baemin maintains a dominant 65% national share, Coupang Eats' WOW membership has seized market leadership across dense Seoul metropolitan districts by delivering within 20 to 25 minutes using advanced integrated logistics . Middle Eastern operations remain highly profitable with Talabat commanding a dominant position in core GCC markets, though Meituan's Keeta poses a major strategic threat by deploying a $1 billion capital commitment in Saudi Arabia . 15 16 indicators show stable central engagement with the group achieving a corporate Employee Net Promoter Score of 48, executive leadership team eNPS of 57, and global corporate office attrition of 10.00% in 2024 . However, the corporate culture matches a highly centralized, Berlin-dominated operational model that undermines regional brands, with convoluted organizational matrix frequently diluting individual accountability and regional teams feeling highly unmotivated . Headcount rationalization saw total staff decline 4.26% to 42,711 in 2024, with delivery and food processing headcount declining 10.38%, tech and product engineering falling 12.89%,

and central streamlining including 400 structural layoffs across Berlin and Barcelona headquarters .
