EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (BLUF)

Careium is executing a fundamental business model transition from a traditional telecare hardware provider to an end-to-end digital health tech provider powered by its i-Care Center (ICC) SaaS platform, presenting investors with a structural growth opportunity underpinned by demographic tailwinds and regulatory-driven hardware replacement cycles across Europe. Interim CEO Peter Heuman articulated the strategic ambition to build a unified, modern European health tech company while integrating historic acquisitions to strengthen the customer offering, a vision now being operationalized under permanent CEO Tove Christiansson, who joined on June 1, 2026, stabilizing leadership after Heuman's rapid interim turnaround phase.

Activist presence has materially shifted, with Accendo Capital holding a 12.10% stake as of December 31, 2025, but its representative Mark Shay being replaced by Louis So of Kunshan Q Technology on the Nomination Committee on January 19, 2026, where Kunshan Q Technology held 4.83% of capital and votes at year-end 2025. This committee displacement suggests a governance orientation change from activist pressure toward longer-term strategic ownership, though the implications for capital allocation discipline remain uncertain given Kunshan's limited public track record in European small-caps.
