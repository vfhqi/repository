9.
Active cycle selection: Management actively manages cycles, shrinking cyber GWP by 8.80% in 2025 to maintain pricing integrity
10.
Broker partner integration: Brokers represent the primary distribution channel, with Beazley maintaining deep partnerships across the London Market
11.
Diversified customer base: The customer base is highly diversified across retail and mid-market commercial segments, reducing concentration risk
12.

13.
