- None — Severn Trent is a standard single-class ordinary share structure listed on the London Stock Exchange; one share, one vote

- Government/Ofwat would have practical veto over any ownership change requiring licence transfer — the regulated-utility licence is the de facto anti-takeover mechanism, not a governance red flag but a structural feature that limits hostile-bid risk (positive or negative depending on investor objective)

Related-party transactions:

Audit history:

- No qualified audit opinion in L3Y Annual Reports

Restatement history:
