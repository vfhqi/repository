Profitability and Margin Evolution
Over the past three years, Elekta's profitability has faced significant pressure from macroeconomic factors, supply
chain disruptions, and input cost inflation. Elekta's Four Must-Win Battles for Profitable Growth
The Elekta Reset: Simplify, Empower, Speed
The first and most immediate priority is a restructuring of the operating model, termed "Simplify, Empower, Speed." This initiative transitions Elekta to a regionally based P&L structure, reducing organizational layers from nine to six to accelerate decision-making and bring leadership closer to the customer. 2022/23. During that earlier period, pressured earnings and substantial research and development

to SEK 815 million. This momentum accelerated through fiscal year 2024/25, culminating in a strong full-year
