EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (BLUF)

Materiality of Leadership and Strategic Change

The company executed two CEO transitions within eighteen months, with Benjamin Revcolevschi departing after only one year in October 2025 and founder Octave Klaba reuniting the Chairman and CEO roles , representing a material governance shift that Morgan Stanley analysts flag as creating market penalty risk. The commercial reorganization under new Chief Revenue Officer Corporate Bruno Ronsse targets high-value enterprise accounts across six European countries, aligning sales execution with accelerating sovereign cloud

demand . The €350 million OPRA transaction was financed through refinancing packages totaling up to €1,150 million, driving average interest costs up to 5.3% and increasing financial leverage despite eliminating private equity overhang . Strategic verticalization created a dedicated Defence vertical built on SecNumCloud-certified products, with specialized recruitment of military backgrounds to coordinate European defense ministries seeking AI-augmented command systems across three certified data centers . The AI Lab expansion through Dragon LLM acquisition and exclusive Gladia negotiations represents a fundamental shift toward higher-margin platform-as-a-service layers, complemented by the massive Alchemy Bare Metal contract deploying over 2,000 servers for global Web3 node infrastructure .

Top Five to Eight Key Drivers

Public Cloud expansion accelerated to 20.2% like-for-like growth in Q3 FY2026, significantly outpacing the group's consolidated 6.9% organic growth and driving margin-rich product mix evolution . The European data sovereignty market valued above €10 billion and expanding at 76% annually acts as a structural demand catalyst, validated by the €180 million European Commission consortium contract and selection for the European Central Bank digital Euro infrastructure project . Generative AI platform buildout through Dragon LLM acquisition, Gladia Speech-to-Text integration, and the OVHai Workspace preview at VivaTech 2026 enables agentic productivity tools with native end-to-end encryption for sovereign deployments . The multi-year Alchemy contract utilizing over 2,000 Bare Metal servers establishes OVH as the primary infrastructure provider for global blockchain node operations , creating a replicable playbook for other high-growth technical verticals. The OPRA capital structure optimization retired 20.4% of outstanding shares and concentrated founder ownership to 81.3%, though refinancing increased average interest costs to 5.3% and net leverage stood at 2.6x as of February 2026 . Proactive supply chain management through front-loading FY26 capex saved €10 million and building €50 million of component stock for FY27 mitigates hardware inflation that multiplied server memory costs sixfold over twelve months . Proprietary water-cooling technology utilizing 20 times less water than industry peers and Smart Racks reducing cooling electricity by 50% provide structural cost advantages .
