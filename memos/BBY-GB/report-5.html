BLUF — Bottom Line Up Front

  • FY2024 delivered a clean underlying beat — revenue £10,015m (+4%), underlying EPS 43.6p (+17%), UK Construction margin expanded to 2.7% for a seventh consecutive year of improvement; direction is unambiguously right.
  • The statutory picture is messier: reported EPS 34.2p vs 35.3p prior year; a £49m after-tax non-underlying charge (BSA £83m + US Civils jury verdict) produced a 17% miss vs analyst headline estimates. ❌
  • Order book reached £18.4bn (+12%), with power transmission more than doubling in the year; the FY2024 backlog has since become the launchpad for FY2025's record £22.7bn. [AS·ARS]

  • Sceptical anchor: Reported EPS declined year-on-year (34.2p vs 35.3p); a shareholder buying on headline underlying numbers faces rising legacy charge risk that could structurally compress reported earnings.
