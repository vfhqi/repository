Business model transformation and ecosystem positioning

1.
Prosus has evolved from a passive investment holding company into an active regional technology operator, concentrating operations across Latin America, Europe, and India through high-growth consumer internet platforms. The massive Tencent associate stake remains the primary economic engine, with Prosus holding a 22.66% economic interest that generates highly predictable cash dividends anchoring the group's valuation. Scaled food delivery assets include 100% of iFood and 22.31% of Swiggy , complemented by the October 2025 full acquisition of Just Eat Takeaway and consolidated Classifieds operations under OLX Group. Revenue is diversified across transactional commissions, online sales, and advertising, with food delivery revenue expanding from 20% to 34% of total sales in fiscal year 2026. The group deploys over 70,000 AI agents globally, utilizing a proprietary Large Commerce Model trained on transaction data from 2 billion customers to unlock operational capacity. Fabricio Bloisi, former iFood CEO, was appointed Group chief executive in July 2024, placing entrepreneurship and active AI-first operations at the strategic core
