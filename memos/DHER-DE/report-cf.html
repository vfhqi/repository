EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (BLUF)

Delivery Hero faces a pivotal inflection point characterized by elevated organizational transition risk as co-founder Niklas Östberg steps down as CEO by March 2027 amid activist pressure from Aspex Management, while simultaneously executing an aggressive portfolio restructuring that is crystallizing substantial asset value through key regional divestments and pivoting strategically to an Everyday App model that is deepening customer lock-in through high-margin multi-vertical monetization flywheels. The leadership transition unfolds against a backdrop of severely deteriorated employee engagement, with the global eNPS contracting to a deep deficit of 22 points in 2025. The activist campaign by Aspex Management, which increased its stake to approximately 14% through a May 2026 transaction with Prosus, successfully forced the CEO transition before Uber subsequently acquired Aspex's entire shareholding at just below EUR 40 per share, lifting Uber's economic ownership to approximately 36.83%.
