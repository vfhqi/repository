Switching Costs and Pricing Dynamics

Retail switching costs are extremely high due to physical terminal replacement and staff retraining, with system migrations typically taking 12-18 months, making competitor displacement highly difficult . Digital iLottery platform switching costs are substantially lower, primarily involving secure database migrations rather than physical infrastructure overhauls . Supply Chain and Distribution Channel Analysis

Reliance on intensive digital infrastructure exposes the company to severe utility price volatility, with high electricity prices potentially increasing group hosting expenses and requiring substantial greener infrastructure investments . The transformational Bally's Interactive acquisition rebalances the group towards high-growth online gaming channels, with B2C digital operations now generating two-thirds of pro forma group revenues . Physical retail networks face disintermediation risks as wagers migrate rapidly online, while rising UK Remote Gaming Duties compress margins for low-scale distribution partners .
