The one piece of evidence that would flip the call is the constant-currency guidance range: if it was also held flat, the whisper-vs-guidance story dominates; if it was raised in constant-currency, FX is the primary driver.

Unexplained residual. The post-event grind lower (~2-3% over the back two weeks) is not fully explained by either candidate. Estimate-revision drift — sell-side models mechanically lowering EPS after a reaffirmation — is the most plausible ongoing pressure, but a positioning unwind (long-only rotation into faster-growth names) cannot be excluded.

Decision prompt: run Q5 earnings delivery first; the guidance bridge (reported vs constant-currency vs whisper) is the single highest-resolution data point.
