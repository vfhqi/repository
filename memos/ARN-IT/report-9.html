  • Cause — weather: Wind load factors in the Italian portfolio show inter-annual variability of ±5-8% around the long-term average — FY23 was a strong wind year, FY24 was closer to average; the FY22 base was approximately average per management commentary [#J]
  • Investor implication: H1 results should not be extrapolated to full-year consensus without adjustment for H2 seasonality — H1 EPS typically runs 44-47% of full-year, so an H1 "miss" vs 50/50 interpolation is structurally expected, not a warning signal [#I]

Sceptical bullet: What's the bearish read on seasonal patterns? Through what mechanism might apparent seasonality be a weakening cyclical pattern misread as structural?
