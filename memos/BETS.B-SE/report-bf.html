EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (BLUF)

Betsson AB operates as a geographically diversified digital gaming operator with its B2C division contributing 84% of total group revenue in Q2 2026 , complemented by a B2B licensing business that provides proprietary sportsbook and platform technology to external operators. The B2C portfolio encompasses flagship brands including Betsson, Betsafe, NordicBet, StarCasino, Rizk, and Inkabet , while B2B operations generate 16% of group sales through the proprietary Techsson Player Account Management platform and licensed sportsbook engines . The business model centers on proprietary technology ownership that delivers structural operating leverage, though near-term performance faces significant headwinds from declining B2B license revenues and escalating gaming tax burdens across newly regulated markets. The group's foundation rests on long-standing leadership continuity under CEO Pontus Lindwall since 1991, disciplined capital allocation favoring shareholder returns, and a deep regulatory moat spanning 23 licensed jurisdictions , offset by severe single-partner concentration risk in its high-margin Turkish B2B segment. 1 4
