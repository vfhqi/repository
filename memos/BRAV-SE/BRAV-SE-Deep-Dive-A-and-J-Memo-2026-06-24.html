B.1 Business Description 7 findings
Business Description -- A plain-English description of what the company does, how it makes money, and its primary market.
1.
Multi-technical building-services installer and maintainer. Bravida installs and services electrical, heating, plumbing, HVAC, cooling, security and fire systems across ~206 locations and ~13,000 staff in Sweden, Norway, Denmark and Finland
2.
Mid-cap Nordic scale, revenue roughly doubled in a decade. FY2025 net sales SEK 28.2bn (~USD 2.79bn), up from SEK 14.7bn in 2016; the largest listed pure-play Nordic technical installer; cohort peer Burkhalter is CHF 1.207bn (~SEK 13bn), roughly half the scale
3.
A defensive service book under a cyclical installation book. Installation 52% / service 48%; service grew +8% in the 2024 downturn while installation fell 3%, the structural buffer that limits the cycle; Denmark's service mix is already at ~59%, the model's target endpoint
4.
Diversified geography and end-market. Sweden 47-48%, Denmark 24-25%, Norway 19-21%, Finland 8%; end-market spread across construction 26%, public 21%, commercial, industrial and property, with the largest client under 5% of revenue and 91,000 clients in aggregate
5.
Asset-light, improving but not exceptional returns. FY2025 EBITA margin 5.9% (vs 7% target), ROE 13.8%, ROIC ~8.2%, capex under 1% of sales, net debt 1.1x EBITDA, cash conversion temporarily 77%
6.
Margin-over-volume operator with a roll-up engine. Strategy is project selectivity plus bolt-on consolidation (166 deals since 2014 at 5-6x EV/EBITA); M&A slowed to four deals in 2025 vs Assemblin-Caverion's 17; management signals H2 2026 re-acceleration
7.
Investment style. A self-help margin-recovery and capital-allocation compounder on a cyclical-recovery inflection, with an electrification and building-retrofit thematic overlay; the cohort peer Burkhalter runs the same logic at smaller scale in the Swiss domestic market
