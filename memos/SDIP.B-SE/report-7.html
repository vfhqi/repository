  • At current revenue base of ~SEK 5-6bn, each 1pp organic growth generates approximately SEK 50-60m incremental EBITA

  • Operating leverage coefficient: ~40-50bp EBITA margin improvement per 1pp organic growth increment [C] [#A]

  • Mechanism to SP (detailed): Organic growth is the re-rating variable; Nordic listed-PE comps (Lagercrantz, Addtech) trade at 20-25x EBIT when organic growth is consistently above 5%; below that threshold multiples compress 3-4 turns [C] [#A]
