Structural growth opportunities in cyber coverage and specialized artificial intelligence risk solutions represent massive long-term expansion vectors , while recent executive transitions demonstrate deep organizational continuity with Christoph Jurecka succeeding Joachim Wenning as CEO and leveraging his proven track record as former CFO . Munich Re's highly integrated model captures margins from primary retail risks through ERGO to complex global reinsurance, while power dynamics shift as traditional reinsurers trim retrocession spend and retain highly profitable property risks in-house .
