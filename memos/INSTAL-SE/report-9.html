Key Takeaways

Prior CEO systematically overpromised on the 8% margin target while profitability collapsed toward 6%, eroding market credibility before founder Per Sjöstrand's mid-2025 return to execute the aggressive "Instalco 2.0" restructuring framework.

Instalco's decentralised 150-unit structure proved structurally inferior to Bravida's centralised model during downturns, with Bravida generating higher revenue per FTE and demonstrating superior capacity management through volatile construction cycles.
