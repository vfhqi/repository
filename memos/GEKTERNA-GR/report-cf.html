EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (BLUF)

Activist presence has recently diminished but historically provided constructive engagement and validation of the transformation thesis. Marble Bar Asset Management increased its holding to 6.42% of share capital on June 4, 2025 , before decreasing below the 5% threshold on January 22, 2026, retaining a 4.93% stake . The activist reduction likely reflects profit-taking following substantial share-price appreciation rather than fundamental concerns, and the retained position suggests ongoing conviction in the long-term value proposition. Corporate governance remains highly concentrated with Chairman and CEO Georgios Peristeris holding a dominant 31.80% stake , ensuring strategic continuity but potentially limiting activist influence on capital allocation decisions. The reconstituted fifteen-member Board of Directors approved in June 2025 with 35.7% female representation demonstrates commitment to governance best practices, although the dual role of Peristeris as both Chairman and CEO concentrates power and could weaken independent oversight . 9 10
