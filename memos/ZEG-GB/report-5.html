  • One-off costs: ~€80-120m in one-off integration and restructuring charges excluded from underlying EBITDA in FY 2024; level and duration of these charges is the key uncertainty [#A]

  • Sub: If charges persist at €80-100m+ annually through 2026, reported FCF is structurally impaired vs underlying EBITDA

  • Sub: Management committed to declining integration costs post-2025; key deliverable to monitor

  • Non-GAAP gap: Adjusted EBITDA vs reported EBITDA gap (~€80-120m) is the most material adjustment; additional D&A adjustments on acquired intangibles add further; gap should narrow as integration completes [#A]

  • Sceptical bullet: Margin expansion could be one-off (driven by headcount cuts delivering initial savings) while structural margin improvement (ARPU rebuild, competitive mix improvement) lags; mechanism: if ZEG-GB cuts €150m of staff costs in 2025 but simultaneously loses €100m of service revenue to MasOrange competition, the net EBITDA improvement is €50m rather than €150m, and the path to €1.4-1.5bn EBITDA (thesis end-state) requires revenue re-acceleration that the current trajectory doesn't support
