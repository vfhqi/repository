  • Cash conversion cycle: Structurally favourable — advance billing + low inventory = negative working capital (cash collected before service delivered); this is a structural feature of utility billing models, consistent across the sector [#A]

  • NWC trend — AMP8 capex front-loading: AMP8 capex ramp creates cash flow inflection — free cash flow (pre-capex) is positive but total cash outflow peaks 2025–2028 as AMP8 programme accelerates; net debt/RAB ratio at ~65% (post-Bristol acquisition, estimated) is elevated vs sector norms of 55–65% [#A]
