solutions designed to improve operational efficiency, safety, and sustainability across industrial processes. The
company operates through three distinct but aligned business segments: Steam Thermal Solutions , Electric
Thermal Solutions , and Watson-Marlow Fluid Technology Solutions (WMFTS). [2] Across these divisions,
Spirax Group leverages a highly differentiated direct-sales model that acts as a substantial barrier to entry and a
