§9 — Sceptical Synthesis

Bearish synthesis verdict: The strongest bearish case against RENE-PT's L3Y earnings history is that it reveals a structurally benign but return-insufficient business — clean earnings, predictable delivery, but with no mechanism to generate the EPS growth required for a 25% IRR thesis absent regulatory step-change [#J]

Reverse-engineered bear:
