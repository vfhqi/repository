EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (BLUF)

Sell-side confidence in Mattson has risen sharply as his aggressive portfolio restructuring and clear capital-efficiency targets have eroded legacy integration concerns, with RedEye increasing Sdiptech's "People" quality score to four out of five following Mattson's strategic review, DNB Carnegie highlighting that the ending of the portfolio restructuring phase is reigniting institutional investor interest, and RedEye noting that the execution of ten divestments has nearly erased perceived governance risks among equity investors. Bengt Lejdström provides institutional-grade financial discipline and stability, bringing extensive financial experience in listed companies with intense acquisition agendas, notably serving as CFO of Lagercrantz, and his Lagercrantz background aligns Sdiptech with top-tier serial acquirer standards, matching the financial pedigree of proven compounders.

Sdiptech maintains a highly independent, six-member board with extensive financial experience, with five independent directors, aligning with standard Swedish corporate governance frameworks, and the board is 83.33% independent, exceeding the Swedish Corporate Governance Code requirement of at least 50% independence. Former CEO Jakob Holm remains non-independent due to his previous executive tenure and ties to major shareholder Ashkan Pouya, and Holm's board seat represents a direct connection to Pouya, who controls 14.73% of Sdiptech's voting rights. Executive compensation structure is highly aligned and reasonable, though the board's low non-executive equity ownership and the presence of non-independent founder ties could weaken oversight if acquisitions underperform.

Expert networks affirm Sdiptech's successful product pivot and effective earnout incentives, with a Former Sdiptech Board Member praising the shift from service to product-based acquisitions in August 2025, noting this strategic evolution resulted in better-performing subsidiaries compared to historical service units, and stating Sdiptech maintains a highly selective, exceptionally strong M&A pipeline. Sdiptech's extreme hands-off decentralized structure might allow operational deterioration to remain unnoticed until earnouts expire and founders exit.
