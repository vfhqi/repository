EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (BLUF)

Kerry Group plc has completed an orderly transformation into a pure-play global business-to-business taste and nutrition company, shedding its historical dairy operations to sharpen strategic focus. The materiality of change at Kerry is high and fundamentally constructive , driven by the completion of the Kerry Dairy Ireland disposal, the launch of the Accelerate 2.0 operational excellence program targeting €100 million in recurring annual savings by 2028 , and a decisive pivot toward shareholder returns via a new €300 million share buyback program . Leadership remains stable under CEO Edmond Scanlon, though operational employee turnover spiked to 29.3% in 2025 due to the structural dairy division disposal and plant optimizations . The net signal points to a specialty self-help cost program combined with a volume recovery model that positions Kerry as a highly defensive earnings compounder within the ingredients sector.
