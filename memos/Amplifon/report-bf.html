EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (BLUF) 31 findings

Amplifon dominates global hearing retail with over 10,100 points of sale but is executing a defensive pivot toward full vertical integration through the pending €2.3 billion acquisition of GN Hearing, transforming from a pure-play retailer into an integrated audiology platform . This strategic inflection introduces material near-term stock overhang from equity dilution and formidable execution risk if independent audiologists shift orders away from the newly integrated GN wholesale business . The company operates in a structurally attractive, demographically-driven market but faces margin compression from the terminated US managed care contract, heightened leverage, and severe channel conflict as retail competitors become manufacturing suppliers.
