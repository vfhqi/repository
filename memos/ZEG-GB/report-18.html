J-front verdict: Pre-Vodafone Spain chapter shows consistent deal-driven value creation; post-acquisition operating delivery is at Day 500 and genuinely unresolved — the thesis is intact but unproven at operating level.

Tenure at Zegona Communications: ~12 years (co-founder 2013, continuously Executive Chairman/CEO to date)

Zegona's evolution across chapters:

- Zegona acquired a ~10% stake in ONO, the Spanish cable operator, in 2015 for c.£49m; ONO had already been acquired by Vodafone in 2014 — Zegona's actual investment was in a listed vehicle structure (Vodafone Spain's antecedent structure); this chapter involved a preferred equity instrument in the Vodafone Spain cable business
