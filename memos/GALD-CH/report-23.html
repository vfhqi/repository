Richard's instinct on who wins. Can be a sector, a company type, or a named stock. Example: "Prime defence contractors (BAE, Rheinmetall, Leonardo), radar/electronics sub-suppliers, ammunition producers."

Richard's instinct on who loses. Example: "Defence-adjacent dual-use companies losing civilian volume to defence demand, or companies with European government procurement exposure being crowded out."

Optional (enhancing, not blocking)

  • Market cap preference: Within $5-50bn default, any sub-segment preference
  • Time horizon emphasis: 6M trading thesis vs 18-24M fundamental rerating
  • Stocks already on the Live List or Short List to prioritise in example selection
  • Prior thematic reports to reference for consistency of framework
