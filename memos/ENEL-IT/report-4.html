§9 — Sceptical Synthesis (Cross-Cutting)

  • Reverse-engineered bear: The bear case is coherent and has multiple reinforcing legs: Italian regulatory risk + LatAm FX + rate normalisation stall + capex deployment delays = EBITDA misses guidance in FY25-FY26, debt reduction fails, and the stock de-rates toward the lower end of the peer EV/EBITDA range [#J]
