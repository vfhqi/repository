EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (BLUF) 1 findings

The comprehensive analysis of ALSO Holding AG reveals a transformational period characterized by aggressive geographic expansion , cloud platform scaling , and strategic operational restructuring that positions the company as Europe's largest technology provider . The most recent Q1 2026 earnings delivery demonstrated exceptional momentum , with consolidated net profit surging 69% year-on-year , net sales climbing to 4.3 billion euros (up 31%), and operating EBITDA jumping 46% to 89 million euros . However, this impressive headline growth masks critical underlying dynamics that warrant careful examination.

The peer-relative analysis confirms ALSO sits in the top quartile of European IT distributors , with FY25 revenue expansion of 39.05% significantly outpacing the sector median of under 5% growth . The shareholder structure remains exceptionally stable , with Droege Group maintaining majority control and UBS Fund Management holding a 5.47% stake , while no activist presence has emerged to challenge strategic direction . Yet the complete absence of gender diversity across the Board and Group Management represents a material governance weakness that fails Swiss Code representation guidelines .
