EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (BLUF) 26 findings

Monte-Carlo SBM has undergone material strategic transformation since 2022, marked by the divestiture of its €850 million Betclic Everest Group stake , installation of growth-oriented leadership under CEO Stéphane Valeri , international expansion through the Monte-Carlo One - Courchevel acquisition , and completion of a €25 million annual cost-reduction program . The company operates as a highly concentrated state-backed luxury operator with zero activist presence, delivering resilient financial performance through ultra-high-net-worth customer exposure, though earnings remain subject to volatile casino gaming luck and intensive capital deployment across domestic and international hotel renovations.
