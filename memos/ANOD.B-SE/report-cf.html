EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (BLUF)

Addnode Group operates under highly stable executive leadership, with long-tenured CEO Johan Andersson steering the company since 2017 and Board governance showing structured renewal through the election of Malin Stråhle to replace Johanna Frelin at the 2026 AGM . However, the company faces material near-term headwinds from Autodesk's revised compensation model, which reduced renewal commissions and inflicted a SEK 20m negative headwind on sales in Q2 2026 , alongside severe pressure from the weak German automotive industry, which continues to delay major PLM investment decisions . The net signal on materiality of change is moderately negative in the immediate term but structurally constructive over a three-year horizon, contingent on successful execution of the SEK 100m Symetri cost-saving program targeting full realization by 2027 and normalization of Autodesk contract mix dynamics starting in 2027.
