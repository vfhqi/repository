Core business. Nemetschek Group is a German-listed vertical software group serving the architecture, engineering, construction and operations (AEC/O) market across four divisions: Design (Graphisoft, Allplan), Build (Bluebeam, HCSS), Manage (Spacewell), Media (Maxon)

5.
Geographic mix. Americas 42%, EMEA ex-Germany 31%, Germany 17%, Asia-Pacific 10%; Germany is the historic home market but now trails international growth
6.
Financial character. EBITDA margin 31.2% in FY25 with 108.6% cash conversion; COGS only 3.78% of revenue, cash capex 1.15%, yielding a software-like capital-light profile
7.
Industry position. Top-5 AEC/O vendors hold 38% combined share; Autodesk leads at 18% (4-5x larger); Nemetschek holds ~5% with an open-BIM best-of-breed positioning against Autodesk's closed platform

Ownership. Nemetschek family holds 45.1% of shares; 54.9% free float; no activist presence above the 3% disclosure threshold
