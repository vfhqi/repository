  • Sub-point: Evidence supporting rebuttal: deal count in 2023-24 below 2022 levels; management has not provided a credible public pipeline visibility update

  • Sub-point: Cultural cohesion degrades as subsidiary count exceeds 30-40; management bandwidth risk is real and unaddressed by bulls

  • Bull thesis #3: "Returns on capital are exceptional — TEQ-SE buys at 5-8× EBIT and the multiple is immediately re-rated to 20× in the group structure"

  • Sub-point: If TEQ-SE pays 9× and the group re-rates to 18× (bear-case scenario), the arbitrage is 2× not the historic 4× — materially reducing capital deployment ROI

  • Sub-point: This is a base bear scenario, not a catastrophe; the business does not fail, it re-rates to a more pedestrian acquirer multiple
