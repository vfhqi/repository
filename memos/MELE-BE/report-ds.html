7.

10.
Sourcing lag – structural delay: As an IC designer sourcing from X-FAB, Melexis sits one step further removed from direct OEM demand, causing its cyclical inventory digestion to lag direct peers by 1-2 quarters
11.
Inventory holding – active buffer: Melexis actively held elevated inventory of €292.2m to support short-cycle ordering patterns from automotive Tier 1 customers

Growth Drivers and Emerging Portfolios

12.
Robotics portfolio – outer-year option: Although management highlighted the Tactaxis force-sensing technology for humanoid robots, near-term revenue contribution remains negligible and is expected to become visible only beyond 2027
13.
Datacenter cooling – emerging pipeline: Melexis launched a new motor driver for server and AI GPU cooling applications, though current sales represent only a few million euros and remain immaterial to the 2026 earnings debate

Other side of the story: The structural lag at X-FAB could mean that if automotive OEMs prolong their inventory digestion, Melexis's expected second-half volume inflection will be pushed out into 2027, leaving consensus FY26 EBIT estimates of €160m highly vulnerable
