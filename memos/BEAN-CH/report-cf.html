Materiality of Change

Belimo's business model is experiencing material structural transformation driven by the elevation of data centers from an opportunistic vertical to a formal strategic pillar, with data center cooling revenues expanding to 17.0% of total group sales in 2025 from 11.0% in 2024, while traditional building automation growth remains resilient at high-teens local currency despite depressed European commercial construction markets . The strategic shift is anchored by surging AI infrastructure build-out , which represents the single largest revenue driver for forward earnings, with data center sales growing 76.0% year-on-year in 2025 . Board-level governance changes enhance strategic focus through increased female representation from 25.0% to 42.9% and the appointment of Dr. Karina Rigby, whose deep expertise in critical systems aligns directly with accelerating data center cooling business . The RetroFIT+ initiative successfully addresses skilled trades bottlenecks in the renovation market, offsetting depressed global non-residential new construction activity and establishing a defensive growth pillar . Despite external competitive pressures from larger building management system providers, Belimo maintains a highly defensive 24% global market share in device-level hardware, shielding margins from system-layer commoditization .
