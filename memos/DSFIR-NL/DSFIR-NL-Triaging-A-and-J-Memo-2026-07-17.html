What DSM-Firmenich does. The company manufactures and combines specialty flavours, fragrances, cultures and nutritional ingredients (human milk oligosaccharides, vitamins, biomedical polymers) for global consumer-products manufacturers, formed by the 2023 merger of Royal DSM and Firmenich
2.
Primary markets. Continuing operations are organised into three consumer-facing units: Perfumery & Beauty (fine and consumer fragrances), Taste, Texture & Health (flavours, enzymes, cultures), and Health, Nutrition & Care (dietary supplements, early-life nutrition, biomedical)
