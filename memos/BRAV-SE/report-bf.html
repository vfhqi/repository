EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (BLUF) 21 findings

Bravida Holding operates as a comprehensive multi-technical services provider spanning electrical, heating, plumbing, HVAC, and security domains across 206 locations in Sweden, Norway, Denmark, and Finland, employing approximately 13,000 staff while delivering end-to-end lifecycle solutions from initial consulting through final installation and ongoing facility maintenance . The Nordic building services market remains exceptionally fragmented , with over 25,000 individual vendors controlling the space and the top five multi- technical operators commanding only an estimated 18% of total market share , presenting a structural consolidation runway as the industry entered an accelerating M&A phase around 2010, with serial acquirers continuously rolling up local operators at attractive mid-single-digit EBITA multiples before extracting operational synergies . The business model couples highly defensive recurring service revenue with structural end-market tailwinds driven by electrification, digitalisation, and stringent energy-efficiency regulations, though recent regional installation weakness forced significant structural reorganisation and exposed vulnerabilities in the decentralised operating model.
