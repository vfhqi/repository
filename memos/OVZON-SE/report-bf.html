EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (BLUF)

Business Foundation and Revenue Model

Ovzon's geostationary Ovzon 3 satellite launched on January 3, 2024 and commenced commercial service on July 5, 2024, serving as the central cornerstone of the platform . The satellite features five fully steerable user beams and a reprogrammable On-Board-Processor enabling secure, single-hop terminal-to-terminal communications that reduce latency by up to 50 percent . The newly launched Ovzon T8 terminal is the smallest On-The-Move terminal in its class, designed for seamless integration on unmanned systems, land vehicles, and sea vessels . Recurring satellite services generate superior profit margins while hardware terminal sales are transactional, resulting in significant quarter-on-quarter revenue fluctuations .

Financial Performance and Capital Structure

Industry Structure and Competitive Position

The global satellite communications services market is projected to grow at an 18 percent annual rate to reach 33.10 billion USD by 2030 from 14.60 billion USD in 2025 . European NATO members agreed to raise core defense spending targets to 3.50 percent of GDP by 2035, with the Big 6 NATO-Europe members intending to spend 2.80 percent of GDP on defense by 2029 . The top-5 global satellite operators capture more than 30 percent of industry market share, dominated by SpaceX, SES, Eutelsat, Viasat, and Telesat . Ovzon operates as a vertically integrated SATCOM-as-a-Service specialist with proprietary satellite on-board processing, custom mobile terminals, and secure ground gateways to optimize performance over traditional commodity resellers .

Supplier Dependencies and Channel Remediation

Ovzon remains exposed to single-source component bottlenecks and supplier financial instability driven by global geopolitical turbulence . The establishment of an internal mobile satellite terminal Center of Excellence in 2025 manages proprietary terminal design, software development, final assembly, and fully designed the nextgeneration Ovzon T8 . Falling global bandwidth pricing from rapid LEO constellation deployment has shifted bargaining power downstream, allowing Ovzon to expand services gross margins on hybrid networks . Severe channel concentration materialized when former Italian distributor Gomedia entered insolvency, forcing Ovzon to fully provision 80 MSEK in expected credit losses and heavily impacting historical profitability with 50

MSEK provisions in 2022 and 7 MSEK in Q3 2023 . 20

Operator Quality and Technical Moats

CEO Per Norén brings over 30 years of commercial experience in aerospace and defense, with proven track records at Boeing, Anuvu, and Carmen Systems, while maintaining deep defense alignment as a board member of the Swedish Security and Defence Industry Association . Three High North Expeditions successfully validated uninterrupted geostationary SATCOM performance nearly 1,300 km inside the Arctic Circle, overcoming extreme curvature challenges where typical systems suffer complete signal degradation . Pre-tax ROCE turned positive in 2025 at 8.0 percent, with broker estimates projecting it to reach 14.0 percent in 2026e and 13.2 percent in 2027e . Converting pipeline opportunities requires heroic management dynamism due to long government procurement timelines, while tight banking covenants under the 600 MSEK credit facility severely restrict nearterm strategic flexibility and evaluating two new satellites will introduce massive dilutive future funding requirements .
