  • Margin direction: EBITDA margin expanded c.150–200bps across L3Y (FY22 to FY24), driven structurally by cloud product mix-shift (higher-margin UCaaS displacing lower-margin resale), with further runway visible in the Spanish/German expansion ramp [#J]

  • Sub: This is a structural not a weather or production-driven pattern — unlikely to change as Gamma scales [#I]

  • Investment implication: H1 results should always be read with H2-skew context; an H1 result in line or slightly below expectations does not signal full-year disappointment [#I]
