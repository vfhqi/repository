EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (BLUF)

EDP Renewables operates as a pure-play utility-scale renewable power generation company and stands as the fourth largest wind energy producer globally, with 20.4 GW of installed capacity by the end of 2025 . The operational portfolio demonstrates heavy geographic concentration, with 53% in North America and 32% in Europe , while maintaining technology diversification across onshore wind (64%), solar (24%), battery storage (3%), and offshore wind joint ventures (4%) . Management has pivoted toward a low-risk execution model, targeting approximately 70% contracted generation through long-term PPAs and regulated tariffs, with strategic emphasis on €50 million in synergy savings through corporate integration with parent EDP . The business foundation reflects a disciplined transition from high-growth geographic dispersion to concentrated deployment in A-rated jurisdictions , anchored by long-term corporate power purchase agreements that shield the company from merchant price volatility.
