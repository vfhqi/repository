SOL S.p.A. is navigating a generational transition in financial leadership while executing an aggressive bolt-on M&A strategy, embedding sustainability metrics into executive compensation, and expanding Home Care internationally amid mixed external forces . The investment case centers on resilient 6-8% underlying annual growth in global respiratory therapy markets driven by aging demographics and care decentralization , offset by European industrial gas demand that contracted 6% year-over-year in May 2026 amid persistent manufacturing stagnation .

Leadership continuity remains a hallmark, though Marco Leccese's succession as CFO effective June 1, 2025, brings over twenty years of international M&A and integration experience from the German industrial group Freudenberg , marking a significant generational shift after Marco Filippi's retirement following over 27 years .
