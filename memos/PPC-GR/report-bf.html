EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (BLUF) 40 findings

Public Power Corporation operates as a fully integrated regional utility spanning generation, distribution, and commercial retail sales across Greece, Romania, North Macedonia, Italy, and Bulgaria . The company has significantly expanded its geographical footprint by acquiring Enel's Romanian operations in late 2023 , while executing an accelerated plan to completely phase out coal use by the end of 2026 . The analysis reveals strong defensive positioning through regulated grid monopolies and integrated hedging mechanisms, tempered by intensifying retail competition and heavy capital deployment pressures on near-term cash flows.
