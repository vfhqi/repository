Section 3 – What happened and the consensus deviation 18 findings

Section summary

1.
Sales update – in-line print: On January 22, Galenica published its 2025 sales update showing revenue of CHF 4,135.6 million, growing 5.5% and aligning perfectly with consensus and the upper half of guidance
2.
Earnings beat – margin expansion: On March 10, Galenica reported H2 2025 adjusted EBIT of CHF 234.8 million, beating consensus of CHF 232 million by 1.2%, with adjusted return on sales rising to 5.7%
3.
Guidance beat – consensus upgrade: Galenica's FY26 guidance projects 5-7% sales growth and 6-8% adjusted EBIT growth, which is 1% ahead of consensus estimates and driven by the Labor Team diagnostics business
4.

5.
GLP-1 demand – persistent tailwind: Sales growth was heavily supported by massive demand for prescription GLP-1 weight loss medications, which continued into the first four months of 2026 and cushioned a weak flu season
6.
Dividend surprise – positive hike: Reflecting strong cash generation, the Board proposed a dividend increase of 8.7% to CHF 2.50 per share, surpassing the market expectation of a flat CHF 2.30 payout
