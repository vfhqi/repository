  • Disruption cost: Physical infrastructure maintenance cannot be fully automated — pipe replacement, valve servicing, and cable maintenance retain human-execution irreducibility for N5-10Y at minimum [#A]
  • AI in procurement: AI-assisted procurement by large utility customers could shift negotiating power; not yet evident in contract terms [#I]
  • Adoption curve: Nordic municipalities are late adopters of technology disruption relative to UK/US peers — provides a Sdiptech "slow-burn" protection window [#A]
