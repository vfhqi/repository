§9 — Sceptical Synthesis (Cross-Cutting)

Strongest bear case: TPE-PL's apparent earnings recovery is almost entirely externally-driven and financially precarious — a combination of ETS re-spike, power price decline, or rate shock could simultaneously erase the margin recovery, trigger further impairments, and stress the balance sheet [#J]

  • Reverse-engineered bear: The entire 2024 "recovery" rests on three pillars: (1) ETS prices at €50-65/t vs 2022's €100/t, (2) coal prices normalised, (3) KRM capacity payments on schedule — none of these is within management control [#J]
  • All three could reverse simultaneously in a policy/macro stress scenario (EU ETS Phase 4 acceleration + Russian gas disruption → coal price spike)
