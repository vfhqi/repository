Delivery Hero: A €5 billion conglomerate hiding €10 billion in parts

Delivery Hero SE (DHER-DE) is the world's largest food delivery platform by GMV outside China, operating across roughly 70 countries under brands including Talabat, Baemin, foodpanda, Glovo, and PedidosYa.
