EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (BLUF)

Roche Holding AG operates a highly defensive dual-pillar business model uniting market-leading pharmaceuticals and global-scale diagnostics to deliver personalized healthcare outcomes. The Group generated CHF 61.5 billion in sales during 2025 , with Pharmaceuticals contributing CHF 47.67 billion (77% of external revenue) and Diagnostics posting CHF 13.85 billion (23% of external revenue), while persistent Swiss franc appreciation created significant translation drag limiting reported growth to 2%.

operating profit increased 5% to CHF 21.8 billion with core operating margins expanding by 2.1 percentage points through continuous efficiency initiatives and R&D prioritization. Over the past decade, Group sales grew at a stable 5% compound annual growth rate at constant exchange rates , while core earnings per share expanded at an 8% CAGR , demonstrating resilient through-cycle earnings power.

Roche maintains one of the largest in-house biological manufacturing footprints in the pharmaceutical industry, achieving five-fold productivity improvements in antibody titers that enabled divestment of its massive Vacaville biologics plant to Lonza for USD 1.20 billion . The Group operates 11 dedicated biological manufacturing sites
