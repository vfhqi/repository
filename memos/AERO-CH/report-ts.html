3.
Strategic pure-play restructuring: Following the divestment of its non-core E-Mobility and Energy segments, the company has concentrated entirely on high-value aerostructures
4.
Contract-based revenue visibility: Revenue is driven by multi-year, single-source contracts tied to specific aircraft platform build rates, providing exceptional predictability
