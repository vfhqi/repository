Section summary
  1. Abivax operates a high-value pipeline centered on a first-in-class oral anti-inflammatory small molecule, though its competitive position is challenged by clinical safety concerns and a crowded therapeutic landscape.

Core Product and Novel Mechanism

1.
First-in-class small molecule: Abivax is entirely focused on developing obefazimod, an oral small-molecule drug candidate in Phase 3 clinical development for Ulcerative Colitis and Phase 2b for Crohn's Disease
2.
30 20F Unique physiological modulation: Unlike traditional immunosuppressants, obefazimod selectively upregulates microRNA-124 to activate a physiological brake, broadly suppressing multiple inflammatory cytokines at their transcriptional source
3.
Broad pathway inhibition: This therapeutic mechanism downregulates key inflammatory mediators including TNF-alpha, interleukin-6, and interleukin-17, restoring natural immune balance without causing systemic immunosuppression
4.
Valuable asset ownership: Abivax retains 100.00% of global commercial and intellectual property rights for obefazimod, maximizing its strategic and financial flexibility for future regional licensing deals
5.
Synergistic combination potential: Preclinical models support using obefazimod as a therapeutic backbone for combination regimens, demonstrating synergistic cytokine reduction when combined with S1P modulators like etrasimod
6.
Favourable administration profile: The once-daily oral pill offers a highly attractive convenience profile over injectable therapies, supporting high patient compliance and preference

Value Chain and Industry Landscape

7.
Favoured value position: Positioned as a simple oral step-up therapy, obefazimod bridges the critical therapeutic gap between cheap conventional drugs and burdensome injectable advanced biologics

9.
Durable competitive moat: Robust method-of-use patents assessed by leading global intellectual property law firms provide protective coverage extending into the mid-2030s, which could trigger substantial milestone payments

Rapidly expanding market: The global ulcerative colitis market is forecasted to experience rapid expansion, rising from $9.00B in 2025 to over $21.00B by 2032
