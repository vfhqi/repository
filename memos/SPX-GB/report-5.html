'One Planet' Strategy and Net Zero Progress
Spirax Group’s ESG framework is anchored by its 'One Planet: Engineering with Purpose' strategy, originally
launched in 2021 and refreshed in 2025 to increase focus on materiality and measurable impacts. A
central pillar of this strategy is the commitment to achieving net zero greenhouse gas emissions across
scopes 1 and 2 by 2030. [88] The group has demonstrated exceptional progress against these goals, delivering a
63% reduction in absolute scopes 1 and 2 market-based emissions compared to its 2019 baseline. This
performance successfully exceeded the interim target of a 50% reduction originally set for 2025. [90] Energy
transition initiatives heavily supported this achievement, with 87% of the group's electricity now sourced from
renewable sources. Additionally, the company achieved a 25% reduction in water consumption and a 13%
