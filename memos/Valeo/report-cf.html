EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (BLUF)

Valeo has undertaken a comprehensive strategic and operational transformation under CEO Christophe Périllat following the orderly January 2022 succession, transitioning from the Move Up plan to the Elevate 2028 framework , which emphasizes self-help margin expansion over aggressive volume growth. The company consolidated its Thermal and Powertrain activities into a unified Power Division targeting operating margins of 5.0% to 6.0% by 2028, while the overall group aims for 6.0% to 7.0% operating margins and free cash flow exceeding €500 million . The strategic pivot reflects pragmatic recognition of structural automotive market challenges, including decelerated battery electric vehicle demand across European and North American markets since late 2024 and intensifying competitive pressure from rapidly expanding Chinese original equipment manufacturers and low-cost suppliers . 1 4

Recent operational delivery demonstrates mixed execution quality , with Q1 2026 revenues of €5,120 million beating company-compiled consensus by 2.12% despite global automotive production contracting 3.40% . Gross margins reached 20.20% in FY 2025, the highest level since 2017, while operating margins expanded 40 basis points year-on-year to 4.70% , reflecting successful cost-control initiatives . However, the company experienced order cancellations representing 10% of its cumulative 2022 and 2023 order book, or €7.3 billion, primarily pertaining to high-voltage electric vehicle programs , exposing significant technological execution gaps
