2.

3.
Recurring revenue dominance: Telia enjoys high revenue predictability with service revenue representing 85.8% of total group revenue, driven by mobile service streams providing 43% and fixed services generating 38% of group revenues
4.
Aggressive structural cost reset: The 2024 change program executed a structural reset by reducing the global workforce by 3,000 positions, eliminating no less than 15% of the end-2023 employee baseline and securing at least SEK 2.6 billion in annualized savings
