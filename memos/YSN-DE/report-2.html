Brief company descriptions

Core Identity and Market Position: Secunet Security Networks AG is widely recognized as Germany's leading cybersecurity company and has served as the official IT security partner of the Federal Republic of Germany since 2004. The company combines high-end security products and specialized consulting services to create resilient digital infrastructures. Its core competency lies in developing advanced encryption technologies that are approved by the German Federal Office for Information Security up to the highest national security levels. Over the past three years, Secunet has consistently expanded its workforce to over 1,200 employees, emphasizing technological excellence to address the pressing issues of geopolitical security and digital sovereignty.

Business Model and Value Chain: The company operates an asset-light manufacturing model, focusing its internal resources on software development and systems integration. Hardware requirements are fulfilled through centralized purchasing and collaboration with a heterogeneous network of manufacturing partners. To meet strict security standards, Secunet explicitly prioritizes hardware partners located within Germany or the broader European Union. The company's operations span the entire value chain, encompassing initial security analysis, system design, software development, integration, and ongoing operational maintenance and support.

ARS • 30 Mar

Primary Mission and Value Proposition: Secunet's fundamental purpose is to act as the "guardian of Europe's digital freedom" by protecting sensitive data, applications, and digital identities. In an era of increasing interconnectedness and escalating cyber threats, the company enables public and private organizations to leverage modern technologies while maintaining complete digital sovereignty. This mission is becoming increasingly critical as governments and military organizations seek to decouple from foreign technology providers and establish secure, localized IT stacks.
