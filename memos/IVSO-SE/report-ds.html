2.
Ranked cause - government shutdown: The second ranked cause is the US government shutdown, which is a medium-plausibility driver as it delayed critical US Coast Guard order intake and caused North American sales to fall 55.50% YoY
3.
Ranked cause - competitive pressure: Stiff competition from 3M Peltor in the infantry segment is a low-plausibility immediate cause, as it represents a long-term structural factor rather than a short-term catalyst for the Q1 move

Strongest counter-hypothesis - structural pricing deterioration: The leading counter-hypothesis is that the gross margin compression reflects a structural pricing decline across Invisio's broader portfolio, rather than a temporary one-off donation order
