EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (BLUF) 1 findings

Stuart Neal demonstrates high-plausibility operator credentials through a rare combination of deep payments specialization, proven institutional scaling capability, and exceptional financial execution since his January 2024 CEO appointment, delivering 30% revenue growth to $128.8m and 36% Adjusted EBITDA expansion to $41.3m in FY25 while systematically transitioning Boku from legacy carrier billing toward high-growth digital wallets and account-to-account payments .

However, this positive operational narrative is meaningfully constrained by material credibility tensions including low executive equity ownership at just 0.23% of shares outstanding, net insider selling bias totaling 260,300 shares over eighteen months, highly dilutive share-based payment schemes representing 23.30% of Adjusted EBITDA, and expert network warnings that Boku's services-heavy integration model creates permanent margin ceilings while subscription-authentication friction limits wallet recurring revenue potential .
