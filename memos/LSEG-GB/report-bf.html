EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (BLUF) 32 findings

London Stock Exchange Group has successfully transformed into a global financial data powerhouse, securing highly predictable recurring subscription revenues that protect margins against cyclical trading downturns, though the business model faces exposure to terminal disintermediation if clients transition from Workspace to opensource LLMs connected directly to data feeds. LSEG operates a highly integrated multi-stage model spanning pre-trade data, execution, and central clearing, enabling superior margin capture and deep customer lock-in across the entire trade lifecycle. The group operates with high barriers to entry and strong structural growth, utilizing AI and digital assets to drive long-term expansion while mitigating disintermediation threats, protected by extensive regulatory entrenchment and deep global network effects. 1
