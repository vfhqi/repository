Section summary

2.

3.

4.

5.

Regulatory and Market Risks

4.

7.
Other side of the story: Concerns over supply chain disruptions are heavily overstated because Uniphar maintains an asset-light model that outsources its logistics fleet and passes inflationary costs directly to suppliers
