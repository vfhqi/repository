EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (BLUF)

Business Model Foundation and Strategic Positioning Vestas operates a dual-segment model providing strong recurring service margins that offset the more cyclical capital-intensive turbine manufacturing business , with Power Solutions handling onshore and offshore turbine sales while the Service division manages aftermarket operations across 56,000 active turbines globally . Turbine purchase agreements are almost universally bundled with Active Output Management service contracts, with customers increasingly requesting long-term service agreements of 25 years and beyond . The company is executing a major Operating Model Reset launched mid-2025 to streamline operations, having achieved 900 white-collar redundancies to reduce overhead and improve execution focus. Geographic diversification across

EMEA, Americas, and APAC provides structural insulation from regional policy volatility and localized regulatory friction.
